Proofpoint Tessian

Proofpoint Tessian – AI-native, behavioral email security & human-layer threat prevention

Overview

Proofpoint Tessian (formerly Tessian, fully integrated into Proofpoint since 2023–2025) is an AI-native email security platform that protects against advanced human-layer threats — business email compromise (BEC), targeted phishing, data exfiltration, and insider threats — using **behavioral AI** and **machine learning** rather than signatures or rules. In March 2026, Proofpoint Tessian offers **real-time detection**, **adaptive response actions** (block, quarantine, warn, auto-remediate), **context-aware DLP**, **zero-trust email security**, **integration with Microsoft 365 / Google Workspace / Exchange**, **Proofpoint Insider Threat**, **Targeted Attack Protection**, **CISO behavioral risk dashboards**, and **low false-positive rates**. It is widely used in finance, legal, healthcare, government, and high-value enterprises where BEC and data leakage are top concerns.

Key Value: Proofpoint Tessian delivers human-centric, AI-driven email security that catches sophisticated BEC, phishing, and exfiltration attempts missed by traditional gateways — reducing breach risk, automating response, providing behavioral visibility, and minimizing user friction while integrating seamlessly with Microsoft 365 and Proofpoint ecosystems.

Core Capabilities (2026)

Behavioral Threat Detection

  • AI models trained on normal user email behavior
  • Real-time detection of BEC, impersonation, anomalous attachments/links
  • Low false positives via contextual understanding

Adaptive Response & Automation

  • Block, quarantine, warn, or auto-remediate in real time
  • Adaptive hold & delayed delivery for risky emails
  • User coaching & just-in-time training

DLP & Data Protection

  • Context-aware data loss prevention (sensitive content, PII)
  • Integration with Microsoft 365 DLP & Purview
  • Exfiltration prevention (email, attachments, cloud)

Visibility & Governance

  • CISO dashboards with behavioral risk scoring
  • Incident timeline & forensic evidence
  • Compliance reporting (GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS)
