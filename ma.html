M&A

Mergers and Acquisitions – strategic transactions to combine, acquire, or merge companies for growth, capability, or market position

Overview

Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A) are strategic corporate transactions in which companies combine (merger) or one company purchases another (acquisition) to achieve growth, market expansion, technology/capability access, cost synergies, or competitive advantage. In March 2026, M&A remains a core lever for transformation — with continued focus on **tech-enabled deals** (AI/ML, cloud, cybersecurity acquisitions), **regulatory scrutiny** (antitrust, CFIUS, GDPR/NIS2/DORA), **cybersecurity & data privacy** due diligence, **ESG & sustainability** integration, **cloud/SaaS migration** challenges, and **post-merger integration (PMI)** acceleration. IT leaders play a pivotal role in due diligence (tech stack, security posture, IP), Day 1 readiness (identity, access, data migration), and value capture (synergies, modernization, risk mitigation).

Key Value: M&A accelerates strategic growth, acquires critical capabilities (especially digital/AI), creates synergies, and positions organizations for future markets — but success depends heavily on disciplined due diligence, rapid integration, cybersecurity risk management, and IT-enabled value realization.

Core Phases & IT Focus (2026)

Strategy & Targeting

  • Define strategic rationale (scale, capability, market entry)
  • IT due diligence scoping (tech debt, security, cloud footprint)
  • Valuation impact of digital assets & IP

Due Diligence

  • Technology assessment (architecture, security posture, licenses)
  • Cybersecurity & privacy review (vulnerabilities, data residency)
  • Cloud/SaaS contracts & migration complexity

Transaction & Closing

  • Day 1 readiness planning (identity federation, access controls)
  • Interim operating model & data segregation
  • Regulatory approvals (CFIUS, antitrust, data protection)

Post-Merger Integration (PMI)

  • Integration roadmap & 100-day plan
  • IT synergies (cloud consolidation, license optimization)
  • Culture, change management & employee experience

Key Trends & Focus Areas in 2026

Tech-Enabled M&A

ESG & Sustainability
