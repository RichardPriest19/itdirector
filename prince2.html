PRINCE2

PRINCE2 – process-based project management methodology with strong focus on governance, business justification & controlled delivery

Overview

PRINCE2 (PRojects IN Controlled Environments) is a globally recognized, process-based project management methodology owned by AXELOS. The **PRINCE2 7th Edition** (2023) is the current standard, emphasizing **people-centric delivery**, **sustainability**, **digital transformation**, **hybrid approaches**, and **value-focused governance**. It provides a structured framework with 7 principles, 7 themes, and 7 processes, ensuring projects remain aligned with business objectives, deliver value, and operate under controlled conditions. In March 2026, PRINCE2 is widely used in government, regulated sectors, large enterprises, and public sector projects (especially UK, Europe, Australia), often combined with Agile (PRINCE2 Agile) for hybrid delivery. It excels in environments requiring strong governance, clear roles, stage-gate controls, and auditable decision-making.

Key Value: PRINCE2 delivers predictable, governed project outcomes with clear business justification, defined roles & responsibilities, rigorous stage control, and adaptability to hybrid environments — making it ideal for complex, high-stakes, or regulated initiatives where accountability and traceability are critical.

Core Elements of PRINCE2 7th Edition (2026)

7 Principles

  • Continued business justification
  • Learn from experience
  • Defined roles and responsibilities
  • Manage by stages
  • Manage by exception
  • Focus on products
  • Tailor to suit the project

7 Themes

  • Business case
  • Organization
  • Quality
  • Plans
  • Risk
  • Change
  • Progress

7 Processes

  • Starting up a project
  • Directing a project
  • Initiating a project
  • Controlling a stage
  • Managing product delivery
  • Managing a stage boundary
  • Closing a project
