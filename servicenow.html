ServiceNow

ServiceNow – enterprise platform for ITSM, ITOM, HR, customer service, security & low-code workflows with Now Assist AI

Overview

ServiceNow is the leading cloud-based enterprise service management platform, unifying IT, employee, customer, security, and business workflows on a single intelligent system. In March 2026 (post-Vancouver / Washington DC / early Xanadu releases), ServiceNow is defined by **Now Assist** — generative AI copilots embedded across modules (ITSM, HRSD, CSM, SecOps, etc.), **AI-powered virtual agents**, **predictive intelligence**, **Vancouver platform maturity** (AI Control Tower, generative guardrails, responsible AI), **AI Search**, **workflow orchestration**, **Integrated Risk Management**, and **low-code/no-code development** via App Engine & Creator Workflows. It supports end-to-end digital transformation with strong governance, compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, FedRAMP, SOC 2), and integration with Microsoft 365, Azure, AWS, Google Cloud, Slack, Teams, and third-party tools.

Key Value: ServiceNow delivers a unified, AI-augmented platform that streamlines service delivery, reduces manual effort, improves employee & customer experience, accelerates automation, and provides enterprise-wide visibility — making it the backbone for modern IT, HR, customer, and security operations.

Low-Code & AI Platform
