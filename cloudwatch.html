CloudWatch

Amazon CloudWatch – unified monitoring, logging, metrics, alarms & observability for AWS resources

Overview

Amazon CloudWatch is AWS's native observability platform that collects, monitors, analyzes, and acts on metrics, logs, events, traces, and application performance data from AWS resources, hybrid environments, and on-premises systems. In March 2026, CloudWatch is a mature, AI-enhanced service with **CloudWatch Application Signals** for full-stack service-level monitoring, **Logs Insights** powered by natural language GenAI querying, **Anomaly Detection** & **Contributor Insights** at massive scale, **Container & Lambda Insights**, **CloudWatch RUM** (Real User Monitoring) & **Synthetics** for end-to-end visibility, **cross-account observability**, **AI-driven root cause analysis**, and deep integration with **Amazon Managed Grafana**, **X-Ray**, **OpenTelemetry**, **EventBridge**, **Security Hub**, and **AWS Health**. It is the central nervous system for AWS observability, cost optimization, and operational intelligence.

Key Value: CloudWatch provides comprehensive, real-time visibility and actionable intelligence across all AWS resources — enabling proactive monitoring, rapid root-cause detection, automated alerting & remediation, cost anomaly detection, and — with AI & GenAI features — dramatically reducing MTTR, improving application reliability, and supporting data-driven operational decisions.
