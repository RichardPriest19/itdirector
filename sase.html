SASE

Secure Access Service Edge – converged cloud-delivered networking & security

Overview

Secure Access Service Edge (SASE) is the architectural model that converges networking (SD-WAN) and security (ZTNA, SWG, CASB, FWaaS, RBI) into a single, cloud-native, globally distributed service delivered from the edge. In March 2026, SASE is the dominant target architecture for modern enterprise connectivity & security — widely adopted across mid-to-large organizations and the strategic successor to legacy VPN + perimeter security stacks. Leading SASE platforms include **Zscaler**, **Palo Alto Prisma Access**, **Netskope**, **Cisco Secure Access**, **Cloudflare One**, **Fortinet FortiSASE**, **Cato Networks**, **Versa Networks**, **Aviatrix**, and **Microsoft Global Secure Access** (Entra + Defender integration). Maturity in 2026 focuses on **AI-driven threat & risk decisions**, **post-quantum cryptography readiness**, **global PoP density & low-latency**, **5G & edge convergence**, **unified policy & visibility**, **zero-trust everywhere**, and **regulatory alignment** (DORA, NIS2, SEC 4-hour reporting, GDPR data residency). SASE enables secure, optimized access for hybrid/remote workforces, cloud workloads, branch sites, and IoT/OT while reducing complexity, cost, and attack surface.

Key Value: SASE converges networking and security into a cloud-delivered, globally distributed service — providing secure, low-latency access from anywhere, enforcing zero-trust & least-privilege policies, delivering consistent protection across users/devices/locations, reducing hardware footprint & complexity, enabling AI-driven threat response, supporting regulatory compliance, and — with 5G/edge integration — powering modern hybrid work, cloud adoption, and digital transformation.

Core SASE Components (2026)

Secure Access (ZTNA)

  • Identity-centric, app-level access (no full network VPN)
  • Continuous verification (posture, threat, context)
  • Browser isolation & RBI for high-risk apps

Secure Web & Cloud (SWG + CASB)

  • Inline web filtering, malware/phishing protection
  • Cloud app visibility & control (SaaS governance)
  • Shadow IT discovery & risk scoring

Networking (SD-WAN & Edge)

  • Intelligent path selection & QoS
  • Global PoPs for low-latency routing
  • 5G & edge convergence for branch & mobile

Advanced Security & AI

  • AI-driven threat detection & adaptive policies
  • Post-quantum readiness & zero-trust encryption
  • Unified visibility & policy across all components
