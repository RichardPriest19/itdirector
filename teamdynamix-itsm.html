TeamDynamix ITSM

TeamDynamix ITSM – unified ITSM + PPM platform with AI assistant, asset/CMDB & configurable workflows

Overview

TeamDynamix ITSM is a unified, cloud-based (with on-premises option) ITSM and project portfolio management (PPM) platform from TeamDynamix. In March 2026, it stands out for combining **IT service management**, **project & portfolio management**, **asset & CMDB**, **automation**, **self-service portal**, **reporting & dashboards**, **ITIL 4 alignment**, and growing **AI capabilities** (TeamDynamix AI assistant for ticket summarization, routing, knowledge suggestions). It is particularly strong in higher education, healthcare, government, and mid-to-large enterprises that need both ITSM and PPM in one platform, with excellent configurability, no-code/low-code options, and integration with Microsoft 365, Active Directory, Azure AD, Teams, and third-party tools.

Key Value: TeamDynamix delivers unified ITSM + PPM with deep configurability, strong asset visibility, growing AI intelligence, and excellent value — enabling fast deployment, high customization, strong self-service, and aligned service & project delivery without needing separate tools.
