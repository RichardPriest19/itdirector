Ivanti Neurons for ITSM

Ivanti Neurons for ITSM – AI-enhanced ITSM with unified endpoint management, asset/CMDB & automation

Overview

Ivanti Neurons for ITSM is a modern, cloud-native (with hybrid/on-premises options) ITSM and enterprise service management platform from Ivanti, tightly integrated with the broader Ivanti Neurons platform (endpoint management, security, patching). In March 2026, it features **Ivanti Neurons AI** (ticket summarization, auto-categorization, virtual agent, predictive routing), **built-in asset & CMDB**, **automation engine**, **self-service portal**, **change & release workflows**, **project management**, **SLA & reporting**, and deep integration with Active Directory, Microsoft 365, Azure AD, Teams, Slack, and the Ivanti endpoint/security stack. It supports ITIL 4 processes and is particularly strong in mid-market, government, education, and organizations that need unified ITSM + endpoint/asset visibility without premium pricing or complexity.

Key Value: Ivanti Neurons for ITSM delivers powerful, AI-driven service management with exceptional asset/endpoint integration, high configurability, strong self-service, and excellent value — enabling fast resolution, reduced ticket volume, unified device & service visibility, and seamless support for IT & non-IT teams.
