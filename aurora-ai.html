Aurora AI

IFS Aurora AI – generative AI & intelligent automation layer within IFS Cloud

Overview

Aurora AI is IFS's embedded generative AI and intelligent automation capability, natively integrated across the IFS Cloud suite. In March 2026, Aurora AI has reached high maturity after over two years of production usage — delivering **context-aware insights**, **natural language interfaces**, **predictive recommendations**, **automated decision support**, **generative content** (summaries, job notes, reports, emails), **process orchestration**, **anomaly detection**, and **proactive alerts** within IFS modules (Service Lifecycle Management, Enterprise Asset Management, Field Service Management, Manufacturing, Projects, Supply Chain). It leverages domain-specific large language models fine-tuned on IFS data, IFS industry templates, and customer context — providing high accuracy, explainability, auditability, and compliance readiness (GDPR, GxP, DORA, ISO 27001). Aurora AI is a key differentiator for IFS in asset-intensive industries (manufacturing, energy, construction, aerospace, service & utilities), enabling faster decision-making, reduced manual work, improved service delivery, and measurable ROI.

Key Value: Aurora AI brings generative intelligence directly into IFS workflows — turning complex asset & service data into actionable insights, automating repetitive tasks, enhancing user productivity, improving first-time fix rates & asset uptime, reducing administrative burden, and — with strong governance & explainability — enabling safe, auditable AI adoption in regulated, mission-critical environments.
