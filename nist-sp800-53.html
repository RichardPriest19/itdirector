NIST SP 800-53

Security and Privacy Controls for Information Systems and Organizations – the definitive control catalog (Revision 5)

Overview

NIST Special Publication 800-53 provides a comprehensive set of security and privacy controls organized into 20 families. It is foundational for FedRAMP, DoD, and many commercial compliance programs.

Control Families Include

  • Access Control, Audit and Accountability, Configuration Management, Incident Response, Risk Assessment, System and Services Acquisition, and more
