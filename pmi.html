PMI

Project Management Institute – global authority on project, program, and portfolio management standards & certification

Overview

The Project Management Institute (PMI) is the world's leading professional membership association for project, program, and portfolio management professionals. In March 2026, PMI's **PMBOK® Guide – Seventh Edition** (2021) remains the foundational standard, shifting from process groups to 12 principles and 8 performance domains, with strong emphasis on hybrid approaches, value delivery, stakeholder engagement, and tailoring. The **PMP® certification** (updated 2025) now includes 50%+ agile/hybrid content, while PMIstandards+™ provides digital, living guidance. PMI also offers PfMP®, PgMP®, CAPM®, PMI-ACP®, PMI-RMP®, PMI-SP®, and Discipline-specific certifications (e.g., Construction, Agile). Key 2026 trends include AI-assisted project management, sustainability/ESG integration, value stream focus, and alignment with OKRs, team topologies, and modern delivery models.

Key Value: PMI provides universally recognized standards (PMBOK®), globally respected certifications (PMP®), and practical guidance that improve project success rates, standardize delivery, support career development, and help organizations align projects with strategic business outcomes — especially in hybrid, AI-enabled, and value-focused environments.

Core Frameworks & Certifications (2026)

PMBOK® Guide – Seventh Edition

  • 12 Principles (stewardship, team, stakeholders, value, systems thinking, leadership, tailoring, quality, complexity, risk, adaptability, change)
  • 8 Performance Domains (stakeholders, team, development approach/lifecycle, planning, project work, delivery, measurement, uncertainty)
  • Focus on tailoring & hybrid delivery

Major Certifications

  • PMP® – Project Management Professional (50%+ agile/hybrid)
  • PMI-ACP® – Agile Certified Practitioner
  • CAPM® – Certified Associate in Project Management
  • PfMP® / PgMP® – Portfolio / Program Management Professional
  • Disciplined Agile (DA) certifications

PMIstandards+™ & Tools

  • Digital, living standards library (PMBOK®, Standard for Project Management)
  • Practice guides (Agile, Hybrid, Change Management, Risk)
  • AI & sustainability integration guidance
