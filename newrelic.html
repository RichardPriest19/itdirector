New Relic

New Relic – full-stack observability platform with APM, infrastructure, logs, RUM & AI insights

Overview

New Relic is a leading SaaS-based full-stack observability platform that provides deep visibility into applications, infrastructure, logs, traces, browser/mobile user experience, synthetics, and security posture across cloud, on-premises, hybrid, and multi-cloud environments. In March 2026, New Relic is recognized for its **entity-centric monitoring**, **New Relic AI** (Grok-powered assistant for natural language querying & insights), **Applied Intelligence** (AIOps, anomaly detection, incident intelligence), **OpenTelemetry-native ingestion**, **browser & mobile RUM**, **synthetics**, **infrastructure monitoring**, **security** (Vulnerability Management, IAST), **change tracking & deployment intelligence**, **cost & usage optimization**, and over 500 integrations (AWS, Azure, GCP, Kubernetes, Databricks, Snowflake, MongoDB, Terraform, etc.). It is widely used by enterprises for troubleshooting, performance optimization, incident reduction, and business observability.

Key Value: New Relic delivers unified, entity-centric observability across the entire stack — enabling fast root-cause detection, AI-powered insights & remediation, reduced MTTR, proactive performance management, security visibility, cost optimization, and — with OpenTelemetry & AI — breaking down silos to provide actionable intelligence for DevOps, SRE, security, and business teams.
