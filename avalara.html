Avalara

Automated tax compliance – sales tax, VAT, and regulatory reporting for businesses

Overview

Avalara provides cloud-based solutions for automating tax compliance, including **sales tax, VAT, GST, excise, and other transaction taxes**. Avalara integrates with ERP, e-commerce, POS, and accounting systems to ensure accurate tax calculation, filing, remittance, and reporting. As of 2026, Avalara is a key tool for enterprises operating in multiple jurisdictions, helping reduce manual errors, compliance risk, and operational overhead.

Key Value: Avalara simplifies complex tax compliance by automating calculations, filings, and reporting across jurisdictions — reducing errors, ensuring regulatory compliance, and freeing finance teams to focus on strategic initiatives.

Core Capabilities (2026)

Tax Calculation & Automation

  • Real-time calculation of sales tax, VAT, GST, and other transaction taxes
  • Automatic application of jurisdiction-specific rates and rules
  • Support for exemptions, multi-rate products, and special tax conditions

Filing & Remittance

  • Automated tax return preparation and submission
  • Remittance to local, state, and international authorities
  • Audit-ready documentation and compliance records

Integration & APIs

  • Prebuilt integrations with ERP, accounting, POS, and e-commerce platforms
  • APIs for custom applications and workflow automation
  • Seamless integration with finance and procurement systems

Reporting & Analytics

  • Comprehensive tax reports by jurisdiction, product, and transaction
  • Dashboard analytics for tax exposure and liability management
  • Support for multi-entity and multi-country compliance tracking
