Jostle

Jostle – modern employee experience & intranet platform for communication, engagement & knowledge sharing

Overview

Jostle is a cloud-based employee experience and modern intranet platform designed to improve internal communications, foster company culture, drive engagement, and centralize knowledge for distributed, hybrid, and remote workforces. In March 2026, Jostle is widely used by mid-market and enterprise organizations (especially those with 100–5,000 employees) for its **clean, mobile-first interface**, **personalized news feeds**, **AI-powered content suggestions & summarization**, **employee recognition & shout-outs**, **pulse surveys & engagement analytics**, **onboarding & lifecycle workflows**, **branded digital workplace** capabilities, and strong integrations with **Microsoft 365**, **Slack**, **Teams**, **Workday**, **Okta**, **Azure AD**, **Google Workspace**, and **HRIS/CRM systems**. It competes with SharePoint Online + Viva, LumApps, Workvivo (Zoom), Staffbase, Simpplr, and Haiilo in the employee experience/intranet space but stands out for its simplicity, fast adoption, and focus on culture & connection.

Key Value: Jostle transforms traditional intranets into a modern, engaging digital workplace — improving communication reach, boosting employee belonging & recognition, centralizing knowledge & resources, driving measurable engagement, reducing email overload, and — with AI & analytics — delivering personalized experiences and actionable insights into workforce sentiment and adoption.
