IFS Cloud Modules

IFS Cloud – modular enterprise suite for asset-intensive industries & service operations

Overview

IFS Cloud is a composable, cloud-native enterprise software suite built around modular capabilities tailored for asset-intensive and service-centric industries (manufacturing, energy & utilities, construction & infrastructure, aerospace & defense, field service, facilities management, telecom, MRO, and more). In March 2026, the major IFS Cloud modules have reached high maturity with deep **Aurora AI** integration, **low-code/no-code extensibility**, **mobile-first & spatial UX** (AR/VR/XR support), **embedded analytics**, **sustainability & ESG tracking**, **real-time IoT & digital twin connectivity**, and seamless integration with external ecosystems (Microsoft 365, Entra ID, Azure, AWS, SAP, Oracle, Salesforce, ServiceNow). The modules are licensed flexibly (SaaS-first), support hybrid/multi-cloud, and are designed for composability — allowing organizations to activate only the needed capabilities while maintaining a unified data model, user experience, and governance layer.

Key Value: IFS Cloud modules deliver industry-deep, composable, AI-augmented capabilities in a single platform — enabling organizations to manage complex asset lifecycles, optimize field service, execute manufacturing, run projects, control supply chains, and drive sustainability goals with unified data, governance, mobile access, and intelligent automation.

Core IFS Cloud Modules (2026 Maturity)

Service Management

  • Service Lifecycle Management – contracts, warranties, SLAs, entitlements
  • Field Service Management (FSM) – scheduling, mobile workforce, job costing
  • Customer Self-Service & knowledge management
  • Aurora AI: predictive dispatch, auto-generated notes, next-best-action

Enterprise Asset Management (EAM)

  • Asset registry, hierarchical structures, maintenance planning
  • Predictive & condition-based maintenance (CBM)
  • Digital twins & IoT integration for real-time asset health
  • Aurora AI: failure prediction, root-cause suggestions, work order generation

Manufacturing & Supply Chain

  • Manufacturing Execution & shop floor control
  • Advanced Planning & Scheduling (APS)
  • Supply Chain & Inventory Management – demand forecasting, procurement
  • Aurora AI: production optimization, shortage alerts, supplier recommendations

Projects & Portfolio Management

  • Project planning, resource management, earned value tracking
  • Portfolio & program oversight
  • Risk & issue management
  • Aurora AI: risk forecasting, resource allocation suggestions
