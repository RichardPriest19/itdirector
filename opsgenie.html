Opsgenie

Alert and on-call management from Atlassian

Overview

Opsgenie is Atlassian's alert and on-call management platform, similar to PagerDuty but integrated with the Atlassian ecosystem. Opsgenie enables teams to centralize alert management, automate escalations, and coordinate on-call rotations. For organizations using Atlassian products (Jira, Confluence, Opsgenie works seamlessly with existing tools.

Key Strength: Opsgenie integrates natively with Atlassian products, offering better value for Atlassian-centric organizations.

Core Capabilities

Alert Management

  • Centralize alerts from all sources
  • Alert deduplication and correlation
  • Smart alert routing
  • Alert suppression and tuning

On-Call Management

  • Flexible on-call schedules
  • Escalation policies
  • Team rotations
  • Schedule coverage visibility

Incident Response

  • Create and manage incidents
  • Automatic incident creation from alerts
  • Team collaboration during incidents
  • Post-incident analytics

Integrations

  • 100+ integrations with monitoring tools
  • Jira Service Management integration
  • Slack and Teams notifications
  • API for custom integrations
