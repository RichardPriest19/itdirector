XDR

Extended Detection & Response – integrated, cross-layer threat detection and response platform

Overview

Extended Detection and Response (XDR) is a cybersecurity solution that integrates **endpoint, network, cloud, identity, and application telemetry** to provide a unified view of threats across an organisation. XDR improves detection accuracy, reduces alert fatigue, and accelerates incident response. As of 2026, XDR is a critical component of enterprise security strategies, particularly in **zero-trust architectures, hybrid environments, and SOC automation**.

Key Value: XDR consolidates threat data across multiple layers, correlates signals using AI/ML analytics, and automates response actions — enabling faster detection, prioritisation of high-risk alerts, and coordinated mitigation across endpoints, networks, and cloud environments.

Core Capabilities (2026)

Threat Detection & Correlation

  • Collect and correlate data from endpoints, network traffic, cloud, and identity systems
  • Detect advanced threats, lateral movement, and anomalous behaviour
  • Use AI/ML analytics for real-time threat scoring and prioritisation

Automated Response & Remediation

  • Automate containment, isolation, and remediation across multiple layers
  • Integration with SOAR platforms and endpoint management
  • Reduce mean time to response (MTTR) and human error

Visibility & Analytics

  • Unified dashboards for endpoints, networks, cloud, and applications
  • Advanced analytics for incident investigation and threat hunting
  • Alert correlation to reduce noise and highlight high-risk events

Integration & Orchestration

  • Seamless integration with SIEM, EDR, NDR, IAM, and cloud security platforms
  • Centralised policy management and automated playbooks
  • Support for hybrid, multi-cloud, and on-premise environments
