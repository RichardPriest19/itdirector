SentinelOne

Modern AI-powered endpoint protection platform (EPP/EDR/XDR) known for autonomous threat prevention, detection, and response using its Singularity platform

Overview

SentinelOne is a leading next-generation endpoint security solution that uses behavioral AI and autonomous response capabilities to prevent, detect, and remediate threats in real time. Its Singularity platform provides unified endpoint, cloud workload, and identity protection.

Key Capabilities

  • Autonomous endpoint protection with Storyline™ AI correlation
  • Real-time threat detection and automated rollback/remediation
  • EDR/XDR visibility across endpoints, servers, containers, and cloud workloads
  • Ranger™ technology for active threat hunting and vulnerability management
  • Strong integration with SIEM, SOAR, and identity platforms
  • Lightweight agent with low performance impact

Why SentinelOne in 2026

SentinelOne is frequently chosen by organizations moving away from traditional antivirus toward modern, AI-driven, autonomous security. It is particularly strong in ransomware protection, rapid incident response, and reducing mean time to remediate (MTTR).

Core Modules

  • Singularity Endpoint – Core EPP/EDR/XDR
  • Singularity Cloud Workload – Protection for AWS, Azure, GCP
  • Singularity Identity – Identity threat detection and response
  • Singularity Ranger – Attack surface management and threat hunting

IT Director Considerations

When evaluating SentinelOne, focus on agent performance, integration with existing SIEM/SOAR tools, autonomous response confidence levels, and total cost of ownership compared to CrowdStrike, Microsoft Defender, or other EDR solutions.
