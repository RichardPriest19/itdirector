SafeArc

Enterprise data archiving, eDiscovery, and compliance platform for secure long-term retention, legal hold, and regulatory compliance

Overview

SafeArc is a modern, secure archiving solution that helps organizations capture, preserve, and retrieve emails, files, Microsoft 365 content, and other business records while meeting strict compliance, legal, and governance requirements.

Key Capabilities

  • Immutable archiving with WORM storage and legal hold
  • Seamless integration with Microsoft 365, Exchange, and file servers
  • Advanced eDiscovery and search across archived data
  • Automated retention policies and defensible deletion
  • Granular access controls and audit logging
  • Compliance support for GDPR, HIPAA, SOX, FCA, and other regulations

Why It Matters in 2026

With increasing regulatory scrutiny and the explosion of Microsoft 365 data, SafeArc provides a reliable, tamper-proof archive that reduces risk, lowers storage costs, and simplifies eDiscovery and audit processes.

Typical Use Cases

  • Long-term email and document retention
  • Regulatory compliance and supervisory archiving
  • Legal hold and litigation support
  • Reducing Microsoft 365 storage costs while maintaining full searchability
