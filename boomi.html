Boomi

Integration Platform as a Service (iPaaS) for connecting applications, data, and APIs

Overview

Boomi is a leading Integration Platform as a Service (iPaaS) that enables organisations to connect applications, data, and systems across cloud and on-premises environments. In March 2026, Boomi is widely adopted for enterprise integration, API management, data synchronisation, and workflow automation. Its low-code platform supports rapid development of integration processes, while offering advanced capabilities such as API management, master data management (MDM), and event-driven architecture. Boomi plays a key role in modern digital ecosystems, supporting ERP, CRM, HR, finance, and eCommerce integrations at scale.

Key Value: Boomi enables organisations to rapidly build, deploy, and manage integrations through a low-code, scalable platform — supporting API-led connectivity, data consistency, and seamless interoperability across complex enterprise environments.
