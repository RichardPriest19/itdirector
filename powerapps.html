Power Apps

Power Apps – Microsoft low-code/no-code platform for building custom business applications

Overview

Power Apps is Microsoft’s flagship low-code/no-code platform that enables users to build custom business applications with minimal coding — using drag-and-drop interfaces, pre-built connectors, and AI assistance. In March 2026, Power Apps is a mature, enterprise-grade component of the **Microsoft Power Platform** (alongside Power BI, Power Automate, Power Pages, and Copilot Studio). It supports **canvas apps** (pixel-perfect UI for mobile/desktop), **model-driven apps** (data-driven, Dataverse-based), **portal apps** (external-facing websites), and **AI-infused apps** via **Copilot Studio** and **AI Builder**. It connects natively to **Dataverse**, **Microsoft 365**, **Dynamics 365**, **SharePoint**, **Teams**, **Azure**, **SAP**, **Oracle**, **Salesforce**, and hundreds of other systems. Key 2026 capabilities include **natural language app creation** (Copilot), **governance & DLP policies**, **environment strategy**, **enterprise-scale deployment**, **offline capabilities**, **mobile-first design**, and **compliance-ready** features (GDPR, DORA, HIPAA, SOX). Power Apps is widely used for **departmental apps**, **workflow automation**, **field service apps**, **employee self-service**, and **citizen developer** initiatives while maintaining security, scalability, and auditability.

Key Value: Power Apps accelerates application development by 5–10x — empowering citizen developers and professional teams to build custom business apps, automate processes, integrate systems, embed AI, and deliver mobile solutions rapidly — while providing enterprise governance, security, compliance, and seamless integration with the Microsoft ecosystem, ultimately reducing IT backlog, improving agility, and enabling faster business innovation.

Governance & Security
