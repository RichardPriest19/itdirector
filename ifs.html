IFS

IFS Cloud – enterprise software suite for asset-intensive industries & service management

Overview

IFS (Industrial and Financial Systems) is a leading enterprise software provider offering **IFS Cloud** — a composable, cloud-native suite focused on asset-intensive and service-centric industries (manufacturing, energy & utilities, construction & infrastructure, aerospace & defense, field service, facilities management, telecom, and MRO). In March 2026, **IFS Cloud** is the dominant deployment model (SaaS), with strong **Aurora AI** capabilities (generative AI for insights & automation), **service lifecycle management**, **enterprise asset management (EAM)**, **field service management (FSM)**, **ERP**, **manufacturing execution**, **project & portfolio management**, **supply chain & inventory**, **low-code/no-code extensibility**, **mobile-first experience**, **AR/VR/XR support**, and **industry-specific depth**. It competes with SAP S/4HANA, Oracle Fusion Cloud, Infor, IBM Maximo, and Salesforce in asset-heavy & service sectors, but stands out for its **industry-tailored solutions**, **composable architecture**, **strong mobile & spatial computing integration**, and **customer-centric innovation**.

Key Value: IFS Cloud delivers industry-deep, composable, AI-augmented enterprise software — enabling asset-intensive organizations to manage complex service lifecycles, optimize field operations, improve asset uptime & reliability, reduce costs through predictive maintenance, support digital twins & AR/VR/XR workflows, and — with Aurora AI — provide actionable insights, automation, and faster decision-making while maintaining regulatory compliance & traceability.
