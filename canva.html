Canva

Easy-to-use graphic design platform with AI features, templates, and collaboration tools for marketing, presentations, and social media content

Overview

Canva democratizes design with its drag-and-drop interface, vast template library, and powerful AI tools (Magic Studio). It is widely used by marketing teams, non-designers, and enterprises for quick, professional visual content.

Key Features

  • Magic Studio AI tools (text-to-image, background remover, etc.)
  • Brand Kit for consistent enterprise branding
  • Team collaboration and real-time editing
  • Canva for Teams and Canva for Enterprise with admin controls
