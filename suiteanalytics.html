SuiteAnalytics

NetSuite analytics and reporting platform for real-time insights and decision-making

Overview

SuiteAnalytics is NetSuite’s native analytics and reporting platform, providing real-time visibility into financial, operational, and customer data. In March 2026, SuiteAnalytics delivers modern business intelligence capabilities including dashboards, saved searches, ad hoc reporting, and the SuiteAnalytics Workbook for advanced data exploration. Built on NetSuite’s unified data model, it enables organisations to analyse transactional data without the need for external data warehouses. SuiteAnalytics integrates with the broader SuiteCloud platform, supporting data-driven decision-making across finance, CRM, and operations.

Key Value: SuiteAnalytics provides real-time, embedded analytics within NetSuite — enabling organisations to gain actionable insights, improve decision-making, and monitor performance without relying on external BI tools or complex data pipelines.

Reporting & Dashboards

Saved Searches & Alerts

  • Dynamic saved searches with filters and conditions
  • Automated alerts and scheduled reporting

Platform Integration

  • Data export and external BI tool connectivity
