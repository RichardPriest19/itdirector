MRI Software

MRI Software – real estate & property management platform (incl. MRI Social Housing / Horizon and legacy Capita One / OPENHousing)

Overview

MRI Software is a global leader in real estate and property management technology, offering a comprehensive cloud-based platform for residential, commercial, and social housing management. In the UK social housing sector, it is particularly known through **MRI Social Housing** (which includes the modern **Horizon** platform and the integrated legacy solutions from the acquisition of Capita One / OPENHousing / One Housing). In March 2026, MRI Social Housing / Horizon is a mature, cloud-first solution with strong capabilities in **tenant & CRM management**, **rent & arrears handling**, **asset & repairs management**, **mobile workforce tasking**, **customer & contractor portals**, **compliance & regulatory reporting** (Right to Buy, Universal Credit, equality standards, GDPR), **dashboards & analytics**, and **sustainability tracking** (energy efficiency, net zero goals). It supports end-to-end housing operations and is used by many large UK housing associations and local authorities. MRI continues to invest in **cloud migration**, **AI-driven insights**, **mobile-first experiences**, and **seamless integration** with government systems and Microsoft 365.

Key Value: MRI Software (including MRI Social Housing / Horizon) provides a modern, integrated platform for housing providers — streamlining tenant management, rent collection, repairs & maintenance, compliance, and asset performance — while improving tenant experience, reducing operational costs, enabling mobile working, supporting regulatory requirements, and delivering real-time visibility through dashboards and analytics.
