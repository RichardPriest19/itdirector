uniFLOW / uniFLOW Online – Canon enterprise print management, secure printing & scan workflows

Overview

uniFLOW (now primarily **uniFLOW Online** and **uniFLOW On-premise**) is Canon’s enterprise-grade print management, secure printing, scan workflows, and document capture platform. In March 2026, uniFLOW Online has matured into a fully cloud-native solution with **Microsoft Entra ID / Microsoft 365 integration**, **secure pull printing**, **mobile printing**, **cost allocation & reporting**, **scan-to-workflow automation**, **AI-assisted document classification & OCR** (via Canon uniFLOW Online AI features), **universal print support**, and native compatibility with Canon multifunction devices (imageRUNNER ADVANCE, imagePRESS). It supports hybrid environments, BYOD printing, print quotas, secure release, and compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, ISO 27001). uniFLOW is widely adopted in education, government, healthcare, legal, and large enterprises for print security, cost control, and document digitization.

Key Value: uniFLOW delivers secure, cost-controlled, and automated print & scan management across Canon devices and hybrid environments — reducing print costs, eliminating unclaimed prints, improving document security, enabling mobile workflows, and providing detailed reporting and compliance assurance with minimal on-premise infrastructure.
