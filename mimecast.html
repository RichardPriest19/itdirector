Mimecast

Cloud-based email security, archiving, and continuity solution

Overview

Mimecast is a leading cloud-based email security and management platform that protects organizations from email-based threats while providing archiving and business continuity capabilities. Email remains the primary attack vector for cyber threats, with phishing, malware, and ransomware commonly delivered via email. Mimecast provides defense in depth to prevent threats from reaching users while also managing email compliance, archiving, and ensuring business continuity.

Key Value: Mimecast provides comprehensive email security, reduces phishing and malware risk, ensures email compliance and archiving, and provides business continuity for critical email communication.

Mimecast Core Capabilities

1. Email Security (Threat Protection)

Protect against email-based threats:

  • Malware Protection: Advanced malware sandboxing and detection
  • Phishing Prevention: Detect and prevent phishing attacks
  • Impersonation Protection: Prevent domain impersonation and CEO fraud
  • URL Rewriting: Dynamically scan URLs at click time
  • Attachment Security: Advanced file analysis and containment
  • Spam and Virus Filtering: Block unwanted and malicious emails

2. Email Archiving and Compliance

Manage email records and compliance:

  • Archiving: Long-term email storage with search capability
  • Retention Policies: Enforce retention schedules by user, department, or type
  • Legal Hold: Place holds on mailboxes for litigation
  • eDiscovery: Search and export emails for legal investigations
  • Compliance Reporting: Generate reports for regulatory requirements
  • GDPR Compliance: Support for GDPR right to erasure

3. Business Continuity

Ensure email availability:

  • Email Continuity: Access archived emails during outages
  • Cloud Archive: Continuous email backup and recovery
  • Disaster Recovery: Restore emails after deletion or disaster
  • Mailbox Sync: Restore lost mailboxes

4. Email DLP (Data Loss Prevention)

Prevent sensitive data leakage:

  • Detect sensitive information in emails
  • Apply policies to prevent external sharing
  • Encryption of sensitive emails
  • Custom DLP rules for organization

5. Email Administration

Manage email systems:

  • Centralized email gateway management
  • Routing and delivery control
  • Encryption and signing of emails
  • External mail relay

6. Secure Email Gateway (SEG)

Advanced email filtering:

  • Cloud-based gateway between internet and email server
  • Multiple layers of protection
  • Spam and phishing filtering
  • Advanced threat protection

Mimecast Deployment Options

Cloud Service (Recommended)

  • Mimecast-hosted solution
  • No on-premises infrastructure required
  • Automatic updates and patches
  • Scalable and highly available
  • Faster deployment (weeks)

On-Premises Options (Hybrid)

  • MTA (Mail Transfer Agent) on-premises

  • For organizations with specific requirements
  • More complex deployment and management

Integration Points

Implementation and Deployment

Pre-Implementation

  • Assess current email environment
  • Identify security requirements and threats
  • Plan MX record changes (if cloud gateway)
  • Plan for archive data migration (if applicable)

Implementation Steps

  • Configure MX records to route through Mimecast
  • Set up user accounts and groups
  • Configure security policies
  • Enable archiving and retention policies
  • Integrate with email clients (Outlook add-in)
  • Test and validate
  • Train users and IT staff

Post-Implementation

  • Monitor threat patterns and adjust policies
  • Review quarantine regularly
  • Conduct phishing simulations
  • Monitor archive growth
  • Update DLP rules as needed

Mimecast vs Competitors

Mimecast Strengths

  • Comprehensive platform (security + archiving + continuity)
  • Advanced threat protection capabilities
  • Strong archiving and compliance features
  • User-friendly administration
  • Good integration with Microsoft 365

Competitor Considerations

  • Proofpoint: Similar capabilities, strong in large enterprises
  • Microsoft Defender for Office 365: Built-in to Microsoft 365, lower cost
  • Cisco Email Security: Strong in network-based filtering

Email Security Best Practices

  • Use multi-layer protection (gateway + client + user awareness)
  • Keep email systems and clients up-to-date
  • Implement MFA on email accounts
  • Conduct regular phishing simulations and training
  • Have clear incident response procedures
  • Monitor for suspicious email activity
  • Backup email regularly (in addition to archiving)
