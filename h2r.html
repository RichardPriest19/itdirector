H2R

Hire-to-Retire – managing the full employee lifecycle from onboarding to retirement

Overview

Hire-to-Retire (H2R) is an end-to-end HR framework that covers the complete employee lifecycle — from recruitment and onboarding, through development and performance management, to retirement or exit. In March 2026, modern H2R solutions integrate **HRIS, payroll, learning management systems, performance analytics, and workforce planning tools** to ensure a seamless employee experience, compliance with labour regulations, and strategic workforce optimisation.

Key Value: H2R enables organisations to efficiently manage talent, ensure compliance, optimise workforce planning, and improve employee engagement — while delivering consistent HR operations across the full lifecycle and aligning HR with business goals.
