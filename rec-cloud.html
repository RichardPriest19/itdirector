Rec Cloud

Cloud-based screen recording and video messaging platform for async communication, training, and customer support

Overview

Rec Cloud allows users to quickly record screen, webcam, or both with voice narration, then share secure video messages or training content without large file transfers.

Key Use Cases

  • Internal team updates and feedback
  • Customer support and onboarding
  • Employee training and knowledge sharing
  • Product demos and bug reporting
