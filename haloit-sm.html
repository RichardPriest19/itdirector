HaloITSM

HaloITSM – highly configurable ITSM platform with HaloAI, asset/CMDB & no-code flexibility

Overview

HaloITSM is a highly configurable, cloud-first (with on-premises option) ITSM and enterprise service management platform from Halo Service Solutions. In March 2026, it is recognized for its **exceptional customization** (no-code/low-code forms, workflows, portals), **HaloAI** (AI virtual agent, ticket summarization, auto-categorization, smart routing), **built-in asset & CMDB**, **project & change management**, **automation engine**, **self-service portal**, **multi-tenant architecture**, **SLA & reporting**, and strong integration with Microsoft 365, Active Directory, Azure AD, Teams, and third-party tools. HaloITSM supports ITIL 4 processes and is especially popular in mid-market, public sector, education, and organizations needing deep tailoring without the complexity or cost of ultra-large platforms.

Key Value: HaloITSM offers enterprise-grade ITSM with unmatched configurability, powerful asset/CMDB, growing HaloAI intelligence, and excellent value — delivering fast deployment, high user adoption, strong self-service, and flexible support for IT & non-IT teams without premium pricing.
