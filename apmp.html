APMP

Association for Project Management – Professional certification, competency frameworks & governance standards

Overview

The **Association for Project Management (APMP)** is the UK's chartered body for project management, established in 1972. It provides professional certifications, competency frameworks, governance guidance, and best practices for organizations managing projects, programs, and portfolios at scale. In 2026, APMP continues to evolve with integrations into **ISO 21500 (Project Management Guidelines)**, **PRINCE2 & PRINCE2 Agile certification alignment**, **competency-based assessment frameworks**, **digital transformation & agile governance**, and **enterprise risk & stakeholder management**. APMP supports organizations across the public sector, government, healthcare, infrastructure, finance, and large enterprises in establishing PMO excellence, project governance, resource optimization, and capability maturity.

Key Value: APMP delivers professional project management standards, certifications, and governance frameworks that elevate organizational capability, ensure compliance with ISO 21500, reduce project risk, optimize resource allocation, and demonstrate project management maturity across enterprise portfolios.
