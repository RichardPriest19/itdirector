DOOH

Digital Out-of-Home – programmatic digital advertising on physical screens & signage

Overview

Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) refers to digital advertising displayed on physical screens and signage in public spaces — including billboards, transit displays, airport terminals, shopping malls, retail stores, stadiums, and street furniture. In March 2026, DOOH is a mature, high-growth segment of the advertising industry, powered by **programmatic buying**, **real-time bidding (RTB)**, **audience measurement & targeting**, **AI-driven creative optimization**, **dynamic content**, **weather & context-aware campaigns**, **5G-enabled low-latency updates**, and **integration with omnichannel marketing platforms**. Leading platforms include **Vistar Media**, **Broadsign**, **Ocean Outdoor**, **Clear Channel**, **Ströer**, **LAMAR**, **AdQuick**, and **Place Exchange**. DOOH offers brands measurable, high-impact, location-based advertising that complements digital channels and drives foot traffic, brand awareness, and direct response.

Key Value: DOOH combines the scale and impact of traditional out-of-home advertising with the precision, measurability, and flexibility of digital — enabling real-time creative updates, audience targeting, programmatic buying, performance tracking, and seamless integration into omnichannel campaigns, while delivering strong ROI through increased foot traffic, brand recall, and direct response in physical environments.
