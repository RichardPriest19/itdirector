COBIT

Control Objectives for Information and Related Technology - Comprehensive IT governance and management framework

Overview

COBIT (Control Objectives for Information and Related Technology) is a framework developed by ISACA for the governance and management of enterprise IT. It provides a comprehensive set of controls, processes, and best practices to align IT with business objectives, manage risks, and ensure regulatory compliance. COBIT helps organizations optimize the value of IT investments while maintaining appropriate levels of control and accountability. The latest version, COBIT 2019, introduces a flexible design approach with governance and management objectives, enabling customization based on enterprise needs.

Key Value: COBIT bridges the gap between business requirements and IT capabilities, ensuring IT delivers value while managing risks effectively.
