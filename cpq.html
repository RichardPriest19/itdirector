CPQ (Configure, Price, Quote)

Enterprise software that automates the configuration, pricing, and quoting of complex products and services to accelerate sales cycles and reduce errors

Overview

CPQ systems enable sales teams, channel partners, and customers to configure valid product bundles, apply correct pricing (including discounts and contracts), and generate accurate quotes or proposals quickly. It is a critical component of modern revenue operations and digital transformation initiatives.

Key Capabilities

  • Rule-based product configuration and validation
  • Dynamic pricing engines with tiered discounts and contract pricing
  • Automated quote generation with e-signatures and CRM integration
  • Guided selling and product recommendation engines
  • Analytics on win rates, discount leakage, and quote accuracy

Popular CPQ Platforms (2026)

  • Salesforce CPQ (formerly SteelBrick)
  • Oracle CPQ
  • SAP CPQ
  • Configure One, BigMachines (Oracle), and specialist tools like KBMax or FPX

Why It Matters for IT Directors

CPQ projects often involve complex integrations with CRM, ERP, PIM, and billing systems. IT leaders must ensure scalability, data accuracy, security, and governance while supporting both direct and indirect sales channels.
