O2C

Order-to-Cash – end-to-end order processing and revenue collection process

Overview

Order-to-Cash (O2C) is the end-to-end process that starts with receiving a customer order and ends with cash collection and revenue recognition. It includes order entry, credit check, fulfillment, invoicing, collections, and cash application. In March 2026, O2C is a highly automated, customer-centric process powered by **cloud ERP**, **Revenue Cloud** (Salesforce, SAP, Oracle), **AI-driven credit & collections**, **e-invoicing**, **payment automation**, and **embedded analytics**. Strong O2C performance directly impacts **cash flow**, **days sales outstanding (DSO)**, **customer satisfaction**, **revenue recognition accuracy**, and **working capital efficiency**.

Key Value: An optimized O2C process accelerates cash conversion, reduces DSO, improves customer experience, minimizes revenue leakage, ensures accurate financial reporting, and — with AI & automation — delivers faster invoicing, proactive collections, and real-time visibility into order and cash status.

Core O2C Process Steps (2026)

Order Management

  • Order capture & validation
  • Credit check & approval
  • Order fulfillment & shipping

Invoicing & Revenue

  • Invoice generation & delivery (e-invoicing)
  • Revenue recognition (ASC 606 / IFRS 15)
  • Dispute management

Collections & Cash Application

  • Collections & dunning automation
  • Cash application & reconciliation
  • Bad debt & write-off management
