ROAS

Return on Ad Spend – measuring the effectiveness of marketing investments

Overview

Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) measures the revenue generated for every dollar spent on advertising. In March 2026, ROAS is a critical metric for evaluating marketing performance and optimising campaigns. Modern ROAS management integrates **digital marketing platforms, CRM systems, analytics dashboards, AI-driven attribution, and multi-channel tracking** to ensure accurate measurement, actionable insights, and improved ROI across all advertising channels.

Key Value: ROAS allows organisations to evaluate marketing efficiency, optimise ad spend, and prioritise campaigns that deliver the highest return — providing a direct link between investment and revenue outcomes.

Core Capabilities (2026)

Campaign Tracking & Attribution

  • Track revenue and costs per advertising campaign
  • Multi-touch attribution across digital and offline channels
  • Integration with CRM, e-commerce, and analytics platforms

Analytics & Reporting

  • Real-time dashboards for ROAS by campaign, channel, or region
  • Segmentation by audience, creative, or product line
  • Insight-driven reporting to guide optimisation

Optimization & Strategy

  • Test and iterate campaigns based on performance data

Integration & Automation

  • Automated collection of spend and revenue data
  • Integration with marketing automation and analytics systems
  • AI-driven recommendations for budget allocation and campaign adjustments
