ThemeEntico

Enterprise design system and theme management platform for consistent branding, UI components, and personalized digital experiences across web, intranet, and applications

Overview

ThemeEntico is a centralized design system platform that enables organizations to create, manage, and deploy consistent themes, component libraries, and branded experiences at scale. It is commonly used for modern intranets, employee portals, customer-facing applications, and low-code platforms.

Key Capabilities

  • Centralized theme and branding management
  • Reusable UI component library with design tokens
  • Support for dark/light mode and accessibility standards
  • Integration with Microsoft 365, SharePoint, Teams, and custom web apps
  • Version control, approval workflows, and governance for design assets
  • Personalization and adaptive theming based on user roles or preferences

Business Value

ThemeEntico helps IT and digital workplace teams reduce design debt, ensure brand consistency, speed up development, and deliver better employee and customer experiences while maintaining strong governance and compliance.

Use Cases in 2026

  • Modern intranet and employee experience platforms
  • Unified branding across multiple business applications
  • Low-code / no-code application development
  • Compliance-driven design systems (accessibility, WCAG, brand guidelines)
