VMware Workspace ONE

Unified endpoint management (UEM) and digital workspace platform

Overview

VMware Workspace ONE is a unified endpoint management and digital workspace platform that provides comprehensive device management across Windows, Mac, iOS, and Android. Built on the foundation of AirWatch, Workspace ONE evolved into a broader platform combining device management, identity, application deployment, and analytics. Workspace ONE is particularly strong in enterprise environments with diverse device ecosystems and complex VDI/virtual desktop requirements. It integrates with VMware infrastructure and provides powerful analytics and automation capabilities.

Key Strength: Workspace ONE excels in complex enterprise environments with VDI, virtual desktops, and need for deep VMware infrastructure integration.
