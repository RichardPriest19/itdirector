Unit4

Unit4 – cloud ERP, financials, HR, projects & people experience platform for people-centric organizations

Overview

Unit4 is a cloud-first ERP and business application suite designed specifically for **people-centric industries** (professional services, education, public sector, non-profits, healthcare). In March 2026, **Unit4 ERPx** (formerly Agresso/Business World) leads with **AI-driven insights** (Unit4 Intelligent Assistant), **real-time financials & planning**, **embedded project & resource management**, **People Platform** (employee self-service, experience, HR workflows), **low-code extensibility**, **continuous updates** (no major upgrades), and strong **multi-entity & multi-currency** capabilities. It runs natively on AWS/Azure, integrates deeply with Microsoft 365, Power BI, Teams, and Azure services, and competes effectively with Workday, Oracle NetSuite, SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud, and Infor in the mid-to-large enterprise segment that values adaptability, user experience, and lower total cost of ownership.

Key Value: Unit4 delivers purpose-built, continuously updating ERP and people experience capabilities for service-oriented organizations — combining financials, projects, HR, and AI insights in one platform with fast deployment, high configurability, excellent user adoption, and reduced upgrade pain.
