Jira Service Management

Jira Service Management – Atlassian ITSM platform with AI agents, asset management & DevOps integration

Overview

Jira Service Management (JSM) is Atlassian's enterprise-grade ITSM, service desk, and service management solution, built on the Jira platform. In March 2026, JSM has matured into a full **enterprise service management** tool with **Atlassian Intelligence** (generative AI copilots), **virtual service agent**, **AI-powered request resolution**, **native asset & configuration management** (CMDB), **automation at scale**, **change & incident workflows**, **SLAs & queues**, **self-service portal**, and deep integration with **Jira Software**, **Confluence**, **Opsgenie**, **Compass**, **Bitbucket**, and **Jira Work Management**. It supports ITIL 4 practices, DevOps alignment, employee & customer service, and hybrid IT environments, with strong adoption in software, tech, finance, and regulated sectors.

Key Value: Jira Service Management combines powerful ITSM capabilities with native Jira agility, Atlassian Intelligence AI, asset visibility, and DevOps integration — enabling faster resolution, reduced ticket volume via self-service & automation, improved employee/customer experience, and unified visibility across development and service teams.
