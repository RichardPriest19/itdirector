CREST

International not-for-profit accreditation and certification body for cyber security professionals and service providers, widely recognised for penetration testing and red teaming standards

Overview

CREST (Council for Registered Ethical Security Testers) is a global accreditation body that sets and maintains professional standards for penetration testing, red teaming, and cyber security consultancy services. It certifies both individual practitioners and companies.

Key Certifications & Schemes

  • CREST Registered Penetration Tester (CRT) – Entry-level certification
  • CREST Certified Penetration Tester (CCT) – Intermediate level
  • CREST Certified Red Team Operator (CRTO) – Advanced adversarial simulation
  • CREST Certified Infrastructure Tester (CCT INF)
  • CREST Certified Web Application Tester (CCT APP)
  • Company-level accreditation – For penetration testing and red team service providers

Why CREST Matters in 2026

CREST accreditation is frequently required or strongly preferred for government contracts, financial services, critical national infrastructure, and large enterprise suppliers in the UK, Europe, Australia, and increasingly globally. It provides assurance of technical competence, ethical standards, and quality control.

Benefits for Organizations

  • Assurance that penetration tests are conducted to a consistent, high standard
  • Reduced risk when engaging third-party security testing providers
  • Clear career pathway and benchmark for offensive security professionals
  • Recognition by regulators, insurers, and procurement teams

IT Director / CISO Considerations

When procuring penetration testing or red team services, requiring CREST accreditation helps ensure quality and consistency. Many organizations mandate CREST-registered testers for high-risk or regulated environments.
