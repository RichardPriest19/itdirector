Guru

Team knowledge management and internal wiki platform

Overview

Guru is a team knowledge management platform that serves as an internal wiki and knowledge base, helping teams capture, organize, and share organizational knowledge. Guru focuses on making knowledge discoverable and keeping it up-to-date through verification workflows. The platform integrates with tools teams already use, making knowledge accessible in the flow of work.

Key Strength: Guru makes knowledge discoverable and verifiable, reducing tribal knowledge and supporting onboarding.

Core Features

Knowledge Organization

  • Tagging and metadata
  • Relationships between cards

Discoverability

Verification and Updates

  • Knowledge verification workflow

Collaboration
