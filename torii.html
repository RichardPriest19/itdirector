Torii

SaaS Management Platform – automated discovery, spend optimization, compliance, and governance of enterprise SaaS applications

Overview

Torii is an intelligent SaaS management platform that provides full visibility into SaaS usage, automates license optimization, and helps organizations reduce unnecessary spend while improving security and compliance.

Key Features

  • Automated SaaS discovery across the organization
  • Usage analytics and license reclamation
  • Contract and renewal management
  • Security and compliance posture for SaaS apps
  • Integration with HR, finance, and identity systems

Best Practices 2026

  • Use Torii alongside Zylo or as a primary SaaS governance tool
  • Combine with SSPM for comprehensive SaaS risk management
  • Establish clear SaaS approval workflows and policies
  • Track hard and soft dollar savings from optimization efforts
