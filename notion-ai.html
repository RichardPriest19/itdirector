Notion AI

Notion AI – generative AI embedded in Notion for writing, summarization & workspace intelligence

Overview

Notion AI is the native generative AI layer built directly into Notion workspaces, enabling users to write, edit, summarize, brainstorm, translate, autofill databases, extract insights, and answer questions across pages, databases, wikis, docs, and projects. In March 2026, Notion AI offers **context-aware assistance** (understands workspace content), **Q&A over entire workspace**, **AI-powered templates**, **database autofill & insights**, **tone & style adjustment**, **action item extraction**, **multilingual support**, **team collaboration** (shared prompts, AI blocks), and **enterprise-grade governance** (SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, data residency, admin controls, usage visibility, opt-out for training). It is widely used in product teams, marketing, engineering, knowledge bases, internal wikis, startups, and enterprises for accelerating documentation, brainstorming, and knowledge discovery without leaving Notion.

Key Value: Notion AI turns Notion into an intelligent workspace — generating, summarizing, and extracting insights from your own content with strong privacy controls, brand-safe outputs, and seamless integration — reducing writing time, improving knowledge accessibility, and scaling team productivity while keeping data secure and compliant.
