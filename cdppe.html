CDPPE

Certified Data Privacy and Protection Expert – advanced certification covering GDPR, privacy engineering, and enterprise data protection governance

Overview

The Certified Data Privacy and Protection Expert (CDPPE) is an advanced-level privacy certification aimed at experienced professionals who combine legal, technical, and managerial competency in data protection. It goes beyond the foundational DPO role to cover privacy engineering, privacy-by-design implementation, organisational privacy governance, and cross-jurisdictional compliance at enterprise scale.

Key Value: CDPPE validates mastery of both the strategic and technical dimensions of enterprise data privacy — equipping professionals to architect privacy programmes, implement technical controls, and govern compliance across complex, multi-jurisdiction operating environments.

Core Competency Areas

Privacy Law & Multi-Jurisdiction Compliance

  • Apply GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, HIPAA, and emerging global privacy regulations
  • Manage cross-border data transfers with SCCs, BCRs, adequacy mechanisms, and local law considerations
  • Interpret and implement supervisory authority guidance and enforcement decisions
  • Advise on privacy implications of AI, automated decision-making, and profiling under GDPR Article 22

Privacy Engineering & Technical Controls

  • Implement privacy-by-design and privacy-by-default into systems and architecture
  • Apply data minimisation, pseudonymisation, anonymisation, and encryption at the data layer
  • Design and govern consent management platforms (CMPs) and preference centres
  • Conduct privacy-focused code reviews and security assessments for data-intensive systems

Privacy Programme Management

  • Build and mature an organisational privacy management programme aligned to ISO/IEC 29100
  • Govern vendor and processor management through DPAs, assessments, and ongoing monitoring
  • Develop privacy metrics, reporting frameworks, and executive dashboards
  • Lead privacy incident response, breach notification workflows, and post-incident review

Certification Requirements

  • Advanced-level certification requiring substantial prior experience in privacy or data protection roles
  • Combines written examination with demonstrated practical competency in privacy programme delivery
  • CPD renewal required periodically — aligned to major regulatory updates and professional standards

IT Director Responsibilities

  • Engage CDPPE-qualified professionals for enterprise privacy architecture and programme leadership roles
  • Use CDPPE competency as a benchmark for senior DPO, privacy officer, and data governance appointments
  • Align privacy engineering practices with CDPPE standards across system development and procurement
  • Ensure privacy programme maturity is measured and reported against recognised frameworks
