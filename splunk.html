Splunk

Data analytics and log management platform for security and operations

Overview

Splunk is a leading platform for searching, monitoring, and analyzing machine-generated data across infrastructure, applications, and security systems. Splunk ingests logs and metrics from across the environment, making them searchable and actionable. The platform is particularly powerful for security analytics (SIEM), operational monitoring, and compliance reporting. Splunk is widely deployed in large enterprises for comprehensive visibility across IT infrastructure.

Key Strength: Splunk provides unmatched capabilities for searching and analyzing massive volumes of machine-generated data across complex environments.
