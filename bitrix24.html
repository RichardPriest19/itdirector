Bitrix24

Bitrix24 – all-in-one cloud workspace with CRM, tasks, service desk, HR & Bitrix24 AI

Overview

Bitrix24 is an all-in-one cloud collaboration, CRM, project management, HR, and service desk platform (with on-premises/self-hosted edition available). In March 2026, Bitrix24 is recognized for its **very broad feature coverage** in a single product — including CRM, tasks & projects, service desk, telephony, HR & employee self-service, document management, chat & video calls, calendars, and **Bitrix24 AI** (generative AI assistant for text generation, CRM insights, task suggestions, chat summarization). It offers a generous **free plan** (unlimited users, basic features) and affordable paid plans, making it especially popular among SMBs, mid-market companies, startups, and organizations in Europe, Latin America, Asia, and emerging markets that want a unified workspace without multiple subscriptions.

Key Value: Bitrix24 provides a cost-effective, all-in-one digital workspace that replaces multiple tools (CRM, helpdesk, project management, intranet, telephony) — delivering fast deployment, high user adoption, strong self-service, and growing AI capabilities for teams that want simplicity and breadth over deep specialization.
