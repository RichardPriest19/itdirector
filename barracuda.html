Barracuda

Email security gateway, archiving, and DLP solutions

Overview

Barracuda provides comprehensive email security, archiving, and data loss prevention solutions protecting organizations from advanced email threats and compliance violations. Barracuda offers both cloud and on-premises deployment options, making it flexible for various organizational needs. The platform is particularly strong in email archiving and compliance for regulated industries.

Key Strength: Barracuda combines email gateway security with strong archiving and compliance capabilities, particularly valued in regulated industries.

Core Solutions

Email Security Gateway

  • Advanced threat protection
  • Phishing and malware detection
  • Spam filtering
  • URL filtering and sandboxing

Email Archiving

  • Long-term email retention
  • Compliance and legal hold
  • eDiscovery capabilities
  • User portal access

DLP (Data Loss Prevention)

  • Prevent sensitive data exposure
