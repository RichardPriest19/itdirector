SolarWinds Service Desk

SolarWinds Service Desk – cloud-native ITSM solution with asset discovery, automation & intuitive support workflows

Overview

SolarWinds Service Desk is a modern, cloud-first ITSM and service management platform designed for fast deployment, ease of use, and strong asset visibility. In March 2026, it features **AI-assisted ticket routing & categorization**, **built-in asset discovery & inventory**, **automation rules**, **self-service portal**, **knowledge base**, **change & release management**, **SLAs & reporting**, and seamless integration with Microsoft 365, Active Directory, Azure AD, Slack, Teams, and the broader SolarWinds Observability ecosystem. It supports ITIL-aligned processes while offering a simpler, more intuitive experience than legacy or ultra-complex platforms, making it especially popular in mid-market, SMB, and organizations seeking quick time-to-value without heavy customization overhead.

Key Value: SolarWinds Service Desk provides fast, user-friendly ITSM with powerful asset discovery, automation, and integrations — delivering high agent productivity, strong self-service adoption, rapid deployment, and excellent visibility into IT assets at a competitive price point.
