ManageEngine ServiceDesk Plus

ManageEngine ServiceDesk Plus – comprehensive ITSM & helpdesk solution with Zia AI, asset management & on-prem/cloud flexibility

Overview

ManageEngine ServiceDesk Plus is a mature, feature-rich ITSM and enterprise service management platform from ManageEngine (Zoho Corp), available in both **on-premises** and **cloud** editions. In March 2026, it is known for its cost-effectiveness, deep **asset & CMDB capabilities**, **Zia AI assistant** (ticket categorization, auto-suggestions, virtual agent), **change & release automation**, **project & problem management**, **multi-language & multi-tenant support**, **self-service portal**, **SLA & reporting**, and strong integration with Active Directory, Microsoft 365, Azure AD, LDAP, Exchange, and other ManageEngine suite products (ADManager Plus, OpManager, RMM). It supports ITIL 4 practices, offers high customization, and is especially popular in mid-market, government, and cost-sensitive enterprises that prefer on-prem or hybrid deployments with robust asset visibility.

Key Value: ServiceDesk Plus delivers enterprise-grade ITSM capabilities at lower cost than many competitors, with excellent asset/CMDB management, AI-assisted support, strong on-prem option, and deep AD/enterprise integration — making it ideal for organizations needing comprehensive service management without premium pricing.
