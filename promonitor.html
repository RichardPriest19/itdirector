ProMonitor

Cloud-hosted industrial monitoring & control platform – real-time OT asset oversight, modern SCADA replacement, GIS visualization & proactive alerting

Overview

ProMonitor is a fully hosted, web-based remote monitoring and control system for industrial and operational technology (OT) environments. It provides real-time data acquisition, two-way control, GIS mapping, and intelligent alerting without the high cost or complexity of traditional on-premise SCADA systems.

Key Features

  • Real-time GIS mapping with color-coded asset status (green = normal, orange/yellow = alarm, red = critical)
  • Flexible connectivity – cellular, radio, Ethernet, satellite
  • Customizable alarms, thresholds, notifications & two-way control commands
  • Historical trending, reporting & audit-ready logs
  • Subscription model (no upfront licensing or heavy infrastructure)
  • Browser-based access from anywhere – fully responsive

Best Practices 2026

  • Deploy as SCADA modernization layer for legacy OT/ICS environments
  • Integrate with existing IT monitoring (SIEM, EDR, CrowdStrike, etc.) for converged IT/OT visibility
  • Use for predictive maintenance and downtime prevention in manufacturing, oil & gas, utilities
  • Apply Zero Trust principles and network segmentation (Purdue Model) for secure remote access
  • Combine with FinOps practices to track monitoring-related cloud and connectivity costs
