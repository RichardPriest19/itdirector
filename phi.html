PHI (Protected Health Information)

Individually identifiable health information protected under HIPAA – requires strict safeguards for confidentiality, integrity, and availability

Overview

PHI includes any health-related data that can identify an individual. Organizations handling PHI must implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards as defined in the HIPAA Security Rule.

Key Requirements

  • Access controls and audit logging
  • Encryption of data at rest and in transit
  • Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)
  • Incident response and breach notification
