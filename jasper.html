Jasper

Jasper – generative AI platform for marketing content, brand voice & campaign acceleration

Overview

Jasper is an enterprise-grade generative AI platform purpose-built for marketing teams, agencies, and content creators. In March 2026, Jasper offers **AI-powered writing** (long-form blog posts, ad copy, social media, emails, product descriptions), **brand voice training**, **custom AI agents** (Jasper Jumpstart & Jasper Agents), **multilingual support** (30+ languages), **SEO optimization**, **multimodal generation** (text + Jasper Art images), **team collaboration & approval workflows**, **performance analytics**, and **enterprise governance** (SSO, SCIM, SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, data residency, brand safety guardrails). It integrates deeply with **Google Docs**, **WordPress**, **HubSpot**, **Salesforce**, **Slack**, **Zapier**, and **Microsoft 365**, and is widely used in marketing, e-commerce, agencies, SaaS, and regulated industries for scaling high-quality content while maintaining brand consistency and compliance.

Key Value: Jasper accelerates marketing content production with AI that learns brand voice, ensures compliance & safety, supports team workflows, and delivers measurable performance — reducing content creation time, improving SEO & engagement, and enabling scalable campaigns without sacrificing quality or brand integrity.
