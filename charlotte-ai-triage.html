AI (Charlotte) Triage

Charlotte AI – CrowdStrike generative AI analyst for alert triage & investigation

Overview

Charlotte AI is CrowdStrike's generative AI-powered security analyst, embedded natively in the Falcon platform to assist SOC analysts with **alert triage**, **investigation acceleration**, **natural language querying**, **incident summarization**, **threat hunting guidance**, **detection rule explanation**, **playbook generation**, and **report drafting**. In March 2026, Charlotte AI has reached very high production maturity after 18–24 months of broad rollout — used daily by thousands of SOC teams. It is deeply contextualized on CrowdStrike threat intelligence, Falcon telemetry, MITRE ATT&CK mappings, and customer-specific environment data, delivering high accuracy, **explainable outputs** with source citations, reduced alert fatigue, faster MTTD/MTTR, and measurable analyst productivity gains. Charlotte integrates across **Falcon Insight XDR**, **Falcon OverWatch**, **Falcon Fusion SOAR**, **Falcon LogScale**, and **Falcon Adversary OverWatch**, making it a core component of modern threat-informed SOC operations.

Key Value: Charlotte AI acts as a 24/7 junior analyst multiplier — rapidly triaging alerts, summarizing incidents, answering analyst questions in natural language, explaining detections & TTPs, suggesting next steps, generating playbooks & reports, and — with strong explainability & sourcing — significantly reducing analyst workload, burnout, and response time while maintaining auditability and trust in high-stakes security environments.
