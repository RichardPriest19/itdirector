OpenAir

Professional services automation – manage projects, resources, and billing efficiently

Overview

OpenAir is a cloud-based **Professional Services Automation (PSA)** platform designed to streamline project management, resource planning, time tracking, and billing for professional services organisations. As of 2026, OpenAir is widely used for improving operational efficiency, enhancing project visibility, and optimising workforce utilisation across global service teams.

Key Value: OpenAir centralises project, resource, and financial data to enable better planning, accurate billing, improved utilisation, and real-time reporting — supporting both operational efficiency and strategic decision-making in professional services organisations.

Core Capabilities (2026)

Project & Portfolio Management

  • End-to-end project planning, scheduling, and tracking
  • Resource allocation and capacity planning
  • Budgeting, forecasting, and cost tracking for projects

Time & Expense Management

  • Automated time tracking and expense reporting
  • Approval workflows and audit trails for compliance
  • Integration with payroll, ERP, and billing systems

Resource Management

  • Optimise workforce allocation across projects and clients
  • Monitor utilisation, skills, and availability
  • Support for multi-location and multi-project environments

Billing & Revenue Management

  • Automated invoicing based on time, expenses, and milestone billing
  • Revenue recognition compliant with accounting standards
  • Real-time dashboards for billing, AR, and project profitability
