Nexthink SMÉ

Digital Employee Experience – monitoring, analysing, and optimising end-user IT experience

Overview

Nexthink SMÉ (Software for Monitoring Employee Experience) is a digital employee experience (DEX) platform that provides IT teams with real-time visibility into end-user devices, applications, and network performance. In March 2026, Nexthink enables organisations to proactively detect and resolve IT issues, improve employee productivity, and optimise the digital workplace. The platform combines **real-time monitoring**, **analytics**, **automation**, and **employee feedback surveys** to deliver actionable insights that enhance IT service quality and end-user satisfaction.

Key Value: Nexthink SMÉ gives IT leaders a single-pane-of-glass view into the digital employee experience, enabling proactive incident resolution, IT performance optimisation, and improved employee engagement, while supporting data-driven decision-making and business continuity.

Core Capabilities (2026)

Real-Time Monitoring

  • Continuous monitoring of devices, applications, and networks
  • Detection of performance issues, crashes, and anomalies
  • Real-time alerts for IT teams

Analytics & Insights

  • End-to-end visibility into digital employee experience
  • Root cause analysis and trend identification
  • Custom dashboards and KPI tracking

Automation & Remediation

  • Automated incident response and self-healing scripts
  • Integration with ITSM tools (ServiceNow, Jira, etc.)
  • Proactive performance optimisation workflows

Employee Feedback & Engagement

  • In-app surveys and sentiment tracking
  • Correlating employee feedback with IT performance metrics
  • Identifying pain points and opportunities for improvement
