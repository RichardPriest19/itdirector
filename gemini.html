Gemini (Google)

Google's most advanced multimodal AI model family, powering Google AI Studio, Gemini app, and enterprise AI solutions

Overview

Gemini is Google’s flagship AI model series (Gemini 1.5 Pro, 1.5 Flash, Ultra). It is natively multimodal, capable of understanding and generating text, images, audio, video, and code with long context windows.

Enterprise Use

  • Google Vertex AI platform integration
  • Gemini for Google Workspace (Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Meet)
  • Strong performance in coding, reasoning, and multimodal tasks
