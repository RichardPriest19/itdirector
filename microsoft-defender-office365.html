Microsoft Defender for Office 365

Integrated email and collaboration threat protection

Overview

Microsoft Defender for Office 365 (formerly Office 365 Advanced Threat Protection) is integrated email and collaboration threat protection built directly into Microsoft 365. It provides advanced protection against phishing, malware, and advanced threats targeting email and collaboration tools. For organizations already using Microsoft 365, Defender for Office 365 offers native integration without additional gateway complexity.

Key Strength: Native integration with Microsoft 365 provides seamless email protection without external gateway infrastructure.

Core Capabilities

Email Protection

  • Anti-phishing and impersonation detection
  • Malware and ransomware protection
  • Spoof intelligence and DMARC support
  • URL and attachment detonation

Safe Attachments

  • Sandboxing for suspicious attachments
  • Behavioral threat detection
  • Dynamic delivery

Safe Links

  • URL rewriting and scanning
  • Click-time protection
  • Custom allow/block lists

Data Loss Prevention

  • Prevent sensitive data sharing
  • Policy enforcement on email
  • Custom data classification

Collaboration Protection

  • Teams and SharePoint protection
  • Campaign views
  • Threat analytics
