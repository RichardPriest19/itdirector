Adaptive Shield

Leading SaaS Security Posture Management (SSPM) platform – now integrated with CrowdStrike Falcon for comprehensive SaaS identity and configuration protection

Overview

Adaptive Shield provides deep visibility, continuous monitoring, and automated remediation of misconfigurations, identity risks, and threats across 150+ SaaS applications. It focuses on SaaS-to-SaaS risks, privileged identities, and compliance enforcement.

Key Capabilities

  • Automated security posture assessment and drift detection
  • Identity and entitlement governance across SaaS apps
  • Threat prevention, detection, and response for SaaS environments
  • Integration with CrowdStrike Falcon for unified endpoint-to-SaaS security

Why It Matters (2026)

With explosive SaaS adoption, SSPM has become essential for Zero Trust and supply-chain security. Adaptive Shield helps reduce shadow SaaS risk and supports compliance with standards like ISO 27001 and SOC 2.
