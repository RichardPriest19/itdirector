OneBusiness

OneBusiness – cloud ERP & business management for SMEs & project-driven companies

Overview

OneBusiness is a modern, fully cloud-native ERP and business management platform designed for **SMEs**, **professional services**, **trade & construction**, **retail & wholesale**, **project-based**, and **service-centric organizations** — especially strong in the Benelux region with growing international presence. In March 2026, **OneBusiness Cloud** is mature SaaS offering **real-time financials**, **project & resource planning**, **CRM & sales pipeline**, **purchase & inventory control**, **HR & payroll**, **automation & workflows**, **Power BI embedded analytics**, **AI-assisted forecasting & anomaly detection**, **e-invoicing & compliance** (Peppol, e-factuur, IFRS, VAT), **mobile access**, and **integration ecosystem** (Microsoft 365, Salesforce, payment gateways, banks, webshops, logistics). It competes with **Exact Online**, **Twinfield**, **AFAS**, **Unit4**, **NetSuite**, **Xledger**, and **Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central** in the SME & mid-market space but stands out for its **unified real-time platform**, **project-centric depth**, **automation-first design**, **user-friendly interface**, and **strong Benelux compliance & local market fit**.

Key Value: OneBusiness delivers real-time, cloud-first ERP tailored for SMEs and project/service businesses — providing accurate financial visibility, project profitability, automated workflows, AI-driven insights & forecasting, full compliance (Peppol, e-invoicing), seamless integrations, mobile access, and — with low total cost of ownership — enabling scalable operations, improved cash flow, better decision-making, and digital transformation without the complexity of larger ERP systems.
