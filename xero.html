Xero

Xero – cloud accounting platform for SMBs with AI automation, bank feeds & global compliance

Overview

Xero is a cloud-native accounting software platform designed for small-to-medium businesses (SMBs), accountants, and bookkeepers. In March 2026, Xero offers **Xero AI** (automated bank rule suggestions, invoice matching, cash coding), **real-time bank feeds**, **invoicing & payments**, **payroll**, **expense claims**, **multi-currency**, **inventory & project tracking**, **fixed assets**, **reporting & analytics**, **Hubdoc** (document capture), **Xero Practice Manager** / **Advisor Tools** for accountants, and 1000+ integrations (Stripe, PayPal, Shopify, Square, Zapier, Gusto, Square, etc.). It supports global compliance (UK Making Tax Digital, ATO STP Phase 2, NZ/IR filing, US sales tax, GST/VAT), mobile apps, and multi-user access with role-based permissions. Xero is widely adopted for its clean interface, automation, and accountant-friendly ecosystem.

Key Value: Xero delivers real-time, cloud-based accounting with strong automation (Xero AI), seamless bank feeds, global compliance, and extensive integrations — enabling SMBs and accountants to reduce manual work, improve cash flow visibility, ensure accurate reporting, and scale efficiently without heavy IT overhead.
