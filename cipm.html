CIPM

Certified Information Privacy Manager – IAPP certification for building and managing enterprise privacy programmes

Overview

The Certified Information Privacy Manager (CIPM) is awarded by the International Association of Privacy Professionals (IAPP). It is designed for professionals responsible for building, managing, and measuring organisational privacy programmes. Unlike the CIPP (which focuses on privacy law knowledge), CIPM focuses on programme management — covering privacy strategy, governance structures, operational processes, and team leadership.

Key Value: CIPM is the definitive credential for privacy programme managers and DPOs who need to move beyond legal knowledge into practical programme design, organisational accountability, and privacy operations at enterprise scale.

Core Exam Domains

Privacy Programme Strategy & Governance

  • Define privacy programme vision, scope, and strategic objectives aligned to business goals
  • Establish governance structures including privacy steering committees and cross-functional accountability
  • Develop privacy policies, standards, and procedures appropriate to the organisation’s risk profile
  • Build privacy into organisational culture through leadership, communication, and behavioural change

Privacy Programme Operational Framework

  • Implement data inventory and mapping processes to understand personal data flows
  • Conduct privacy risk assessments and DPIAs for new processing activities
  • Manage consent, preference management, and data subject rights fulfilment workflows
  • Govern third-party privacy risk through vendor assessments, DPAs, and monitoring

Privacy Programme Metrics & Maturity

  • Define and track KPIs and KRIs for privacy programme performance
  • Assess programme maturity using models such as GAPP and ISO/IEC 27701
  • Report privacy programme status to executives, boards, and supervisory authorities
  • Continuously improve the programme through audit findings, incident learning, and regulatory updates

Exam Details

  • IAPP CIPM examination — 90 multiple-choice questions over 2.5 hours
  • No formal prerequisites — recommended for professionals with privacy programme experience
  • Renewal: 20 CPE credits annually, with IAPP membership required for certification maintenance
  • Frequently paired with CIPP/E or CIPP/US to combine legal and programme management credentials

IT Director Responsibilities

  • Use CIPM as the benchmark qualification for privacy programme managers and DPO appointments
  • Ensure the privacy programme is structured, measured, and governed in line with CIPM standards
  • Integrate privacy programme management into IT strategy, data governance, and vendor management
  • Support CIPM study and certification for IT and data professionals taking on privacy responsibilities
