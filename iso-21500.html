ISO 21500

Guidance on Project Management – international standard offering high-level principles and good practices applicable to any project

Overview

ISO 21500:2012 provides a structured framework of concepts and processes (integration, stakeholder, scope, resources, etc.) grouped into initiating, planning, implementing, controlling, and closing phases. It complements PMBOK and PRINCE2.

Subject Groups

  • Integration, Stakeholder, Scope, Resource, Time, Cost, Risk, Quality, Procurement, Communication
