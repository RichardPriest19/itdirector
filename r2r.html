R2R

Record-to-Report – end-to-end financial accounting and reporting process

Overview

Record-to-Report (R2R) is the end-to-end finance process that captures all financial transactions, records them accurately, closes the books, and produces financial statements and management reports. In March 2026, R2R is a highly automated, data-driven process in most organizations — powered by **cloud ERP** (SAP S/4HANA, Oracle, Xledger, IFS, OneBusiness), **robotic process automation**, **AI anomaly detection**, **real-time consolidation**, **embedded analytics**, and **continuous close** capabilities. It covers journal entries, reconciliations, intercompany eliminations, consolidation, financial close, statutory reporting, and management reporting. Strong R2R performance is critical for timely, accurate, and compliant financial reporting, investor confidence, regulatory compliance (SOX, IFRS, GAAP, SEC), and effective business decision-making.

Key Value: A mature R2R process delivers accurate, timely, and trusted financial information — enabling faster month-end close, reduced manual effort, better decision-making, regulatory compliance, audit readiness, and — with AI & automation — significant efficiency gains, error reduction, and real-time insights for finance and business leaders.

Core R2R Process Steps (2026)

Record

  • Capture transactions from source systems
  • Journal entries & accruals
  • Automated data ingestion & validation

Reconcile & Close

  • Account reconciliations & intercompany eliminations
  • Month-end / quarter-end / year-end close
  • Continuous close & real-time reconciliation

Report & Analyze

  • Statutory & management reporting
  • Financial statements & disclosures
  • Embedded analytics & AI-driven insights
