BitLocker

BitLocker – Microsoft full volume encryption for Windows devices with TPM & Entra integration

Overview

BitLocker is Microsoft’s built-in full volume encryption feature for Windows (Pro, Enterprise, Education editions; automatic Device Encryption on consumer devices meeting requirements). In March 2026 (Windows 11 24H2 / 25H2 era), BitLocker uses **XTS-AES 256-bit** encryption by default, integrates tightly with **TPM 2.0** (required), **Microsoft Entra ID** (formerly Azure AD) for recovery key escrow, **Intune** for policy enforcement, **Windows Hello for Business**, and **Microsoft Defender for Endpoint** for ransomware protection. It supports **BitLocker To Go** (removable drives), **password-less recovery** via Entra/Microsoft Account, **enhanced PIN & FIDO2 support**, **controlled folder access** synergy, and **compliance reporting** (CIS, NIST, ISO 27001, GDPR, HIPAA). BitLocker is a foundational Zero Trust control for data-at-rest protection on Windows endpoints.

Key Value: BitLocker provides strong, hardware-backed full-disk encryption with seamless Entra/Intune management, automatic recovery key escrow, ransomware resilience, and compliance alignment — protecting data-at-rest on Windows devices, reducing breach impact, and meeting regulatory & audit requirements with minimal administrative overhead.

Core Capabilities (2026)

Encryption & Authentication

  • XTS-AES 128/256-bit full volume encryption
  • TPM 2.0 + PIN / password / USB key / FIDO2
  • Windows Hello for Business & password-less unlock

Management & Recovery

  • Entra ID recovery key escrow & silent enablement
  • Intune policy enforcement & compliance reporting
  • BitLocker To Go for removable drives

Security & Ransomware Protection

  • Controlled folder access integration
  • Pre-boot authentication & anti-hammering
  • Recovery key rotation & revocation

Compliance & Auditing

  • Supports NIST 800-53, ISO 27001 A.8/A.10, GDPR, HIPAA
  • Audit logs & BitLocker status reporting in Intune
  • Zero Trust alignment (device health + encryption)
