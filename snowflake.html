Snowflake

Snowflake – cloud-native data platform with separation of compute & storage

Overview

Snowflake is a fully managed, cloud-native data platform built for data warehousing, data lakes, data engineering, data science, and AI/ML workloads with complete separation of compute and storage. In March 2026, Snowflake is one of the leading **data cloud** platforms — running on AWS, Azure, and GCP — with **Snowflake Cortex** (AI/ML functions, Snowpark ML), **Snowpark** maturity (Python, Java, Scala, JavaScript in-warehouse), **zero-copy cloning**, **secure data sharing & Snowflake Marketplace**, **Snowpipe Streaming**, **hybrid tables** (Unistore – transactional + analytical), **Apache Iceberg support**, **multi-cloud replication & failover**, **Snowflake Horizon** (governance, compliance, discovery), and strong integration with **Power BI**, **Tableau**, **Looker**, **dbt**, **Fivetran**, **Matillion**, **Databricks**, and AI frameworks (LangChain, LlamaIndex). It is widely used for analytics, BI, data engineering, data sharing, and enterprise AI workloads.

Key Value: Snowflake provides a single, governed, elastic, and multi-cloud data platform — eliminating ETL complexity, enabling secure data sharing, supporting real-time & AI workloads, offering near-zero maintenance, and delivering massive performance & scale with pay-for-what-you-use economics.
