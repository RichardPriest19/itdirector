ProSuite

Integrated OT platform combining ProMonitor monitoring with advanced analytics, reporting, and asset management for industrial operations

Overview

ProSuite is the comprehensive enterprise platform from the ProMonitor family. It extends real-time industrial monitoring with powerful analytics, centralized asset management, automated reporting, and intelligent decision support for operational technology (OT) environments.

Key Features

  • Unified dashboard combining ProMonitor real-time data with advanced analytics
  • Asset performance management and predictive maintenance insights
  • Automated compliance and regulatory reporting
  • Custom KPI tracking and executive-level operational intelligence
  • Role-based access with full audit trail
  • Seamless integration with enterprise systems (ERP, EAM, SCADA, historians)

Best Practices 2026

  • Use ProSuite as the central OT intelligence layer across multiple sites
  • Combine with ProMonitor for full remote monitoring + enterprise analytics
  • Integrate with IT/OT convergence initiatives and Zero Trust architectures
  • Leverage for ESG reporting and sustainability metrics in industrial operations
  • Apply FinOps principles to monitor platform usage and data costs
