SuiteScript

NetSuite scripting platform for automation, customisation, and integration

Overview

SuiteScript is the JavaScript-based development platform for extending and customising NetSuite ERP. In March 2026, SuiteScript (primarily SuiteScript 2.x) is widely used to automate business processes, build custom workflows, create integrations, and enhance user experiences within NetSuite. It enables developers to interact with records, transactions, and system events, supporting server-side and client-side scripting. SuiteScript is a key component of NetSuite’s SuiteCloud platform, working alongside SuiteFlow, SuiteTalk APIs, and SuiteAnalytics to deliver scalable, enterprise-grade customisation across finance, CRM, and operational systems.

Key Value: SuiteScript enables deep customisation and automation within NetSuite — allowing organisations to tailor ERP processes, integrate with external systems, and improve efficiency while maintaining a scalable, cloud-native architecture.
