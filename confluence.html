Confluence

Team documentation, knowledge base, and collaboration platform

Overview

Confluence is Atlassian's team documentation and knowledge management platform enabling teams to create, organize, and share content. Confluence serves as a central repository for documentation, runbooks, technical specifications, and institutional knowledge. In IT organizations, Confluence is widely used to document infrastructure, procedures, incident response playbooks, and operational knowledge. The platform integrates with Jira for linking documentation to issues and projects, making it a natural choice for organizations already using Atlassian products.

Key Value: Confluence centralizes organizational knowledge, making it discoverable and maintainable, reducing tribal knowledge and supporting onboarding.
