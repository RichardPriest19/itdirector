TrustSec

Cisco TrustSec – software-defined segmentation & group-based access control

Overview

Cisco TrustSec is a software-defined segmentation technology that classifies endpoints and users into scalable security groups (using Scalable Group Tags – SGTs) and enforces group-based access policies across the entire network fabric — regardless of location, device type, or connection method (wired, wireless, VPN, remote). In March 2026, TrustSec is a mature, widely deployed component of Cisco's zero-trust architecture — deeply integrated with **Cisco Secure Access**, **SD-Access**, **Identity Services Engine (ISE)**, **Catalyst switches**, **Meraki**, **ACI**, **Duo**, **Umbrella**, **SecureX**, and **pxGrid Cloud**. It supports **macro- and micro-segmentation**, **AI-enhanced tag propagation & policy recommendations**, **cloud & hybrid enforcement**, **OT/ICS segmentation**, **regulatory alignment** (DORA, NIS2, PCI-DSS, HIPAA zone isolation), **post-quantum readiness** in key exchanges, and **automated policy orchestration**. TrustSec eliminates traditional VLAN/IP ACL sprawl, enabling dynamic, identity-centric, intent-based segmentation with consistent policy enforcement across campus, branch, data center, cloud, and remote users.

Key Value: TrustSec delivers scalable, group-based zero-trust segmentation — reducing attack surface, enforcing least-privilege access, simplifying policy management, enabling rapid policy changes without re-addressing, supporting OT/ICS isolation, providing regulatory zone compliance, and — when integrated with ISE, SD-Access, and Secure Access — creating a unified, automated, AI-assisted segmentation fabric that scales across hybrid/multi-cloud environments with strong auditability and operational efficiency.

Core TrustSec Capabilities (2026)

Group-Based Segmentation

  • Scalable Group Tags (SGTs) – classify users/devices by role/context
  • Security Group Tag eXchange Protocol (SXP) & inline tagging
  • Macro-segmentation (campus/branch) & micro-segmentation (east-west)

Policy Enforcement

  • SGACLs (Security Group ACLs) – group-to-group policies
  • Integration with Cisco Secure Access & ZTNA
  • Dynamic policy updates via ISE & pxGrid

OT/ICS & Hybrid Support

  • OT/ICS device profiling & segmentation
  • Cloud & hybrid enforcement (AWS, Azure, GCP via pxGrid Cloud)
  • Private 5G & edge integration

AI & Automation

  • AI-driven tag propagation & policy recommendation
  • Automated policy simulation & impact analysis
  • Integration with SecureX for orchestration
