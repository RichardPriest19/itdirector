Zendesk

Zendesk – leading cloud customer support & CX platform with Zendesk AI, omnichannel & Sunshine custom data

Overview

Zendesk is a comprehensive, cloud-native customer experience (CX) and support platform trusted by thousands of organizations for helpdesk, ticketing, self-service, and omnichannel engagement. In March 2026, Zendesk is defined by **Zendesk AI** (generative replies, Answer Bot, intent detection, ticket summarization, CSAT prediction), **full omnichannel support** (email, chat, voice, WhatsApp, messaging, social), **Sunshine platform** (custom data objects & apps), **Zendesk Suite** (Support + Chat + Talk + Guide + Sell), **Explore analytics**, **automation & triggers**, and strong integration with Salesforce, Microsoft 365, Slack, Teams, Shopify, and telephony providers. It supports both customer-facing and internal IT/employee service desks, with excellent self-service deflection and reporting.

Key Value: Zendesk delivers fast, intelligent, omnichannel support that boosts CSAT, reduces resolution time, increases self-service adoption, and provides deep visibility into customer & agent performance — making it a top choice for customer-centric and IT support organizations.
