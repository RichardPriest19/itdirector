Proofpoint

Email security, data loss prevention, and advanced threat protection

Overview

Proofpoint is a leading email security and data loss prevention (DLP) platform protecting organizations from advanced email threats, data exfiltration, and compliance violations. Proofpoint combines threat detection, user behavior analytics, and security awareness training to provide comprehensive email protection. Unlike simple gateway security, Proofpoint uses machine learning and behavioral analysis to detect sophisticated attacks that bypass traditional filters. Email remains the primary attack vector for breaches, making Proofpoint essential for security-conscious organizations.

Key Strength: Proofpoint combines threat prevention with user behavior analytics and security awareness to address both technical and human elements of email security.
