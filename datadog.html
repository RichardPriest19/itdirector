Datadog

Infrastructure monitoring, application performance monitoring (APM), and analytics platform

Overview

Datadog is a comprehensive monitoring, analytics, and observability platform providing unified visibility into infrastructure, applications, and user experience. Datadog collects metrics, traces, logs, and user session data from across the technology stack, enabling teams to identify and troubleshoot issues quickly. The platform is particularly popular in cloud and DevOps environments where traditional monitoring is insufficient. Datadog's strength is in providing a single pane of glass for all observability data across complex microservices and cloud-native architectures.

Key Strength: Datadog provides unified observability across infrastructure, applications, and user experience in a single platform.
