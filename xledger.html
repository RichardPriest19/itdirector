Xledger – cloud-native ERP for project-driven & service-centric businesses

Overview

Xledger is a modern, fully cloud-native ERP platform designed for **project-driven**, **professional services**, **subscription/SaaS**, **non-profit**, and **mid-market organizations** that require strong **project accounting**, **multi-currency/multi-company operations**, **real-time financial visibility**, **automation**, **low-code extensibility**, and **global compliance**. In March 2026, **Xledger Cloud** is mature SaaS with **embedded AI** (forecasting, anomaly detection, natural language reporting), **real-time dashboards & analytics**, **global tax & e-invoicing compliance** (VAT, IFRS, GAAP, PEPPOL), **strong integration ecosystem** (Power BI, Salesforce, Microsoft 365, Stripe, payment gateways, HRIS), **mobile access**, and high user satisfaction in project-centric sectors. It competes with **Unit4**, **NetSuite**, **Deltek**, **Sage Intacct**, **IFS Cloud**, **Oracle NetSuite**, and **Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central** but stands out for its **true real-time architecture**, **project & resource management depth**, **automation-first design**, and **low total cost of ownership** in mid-market & service organizations.

Key Value: Xledger delivers real-time, cloud-native ERP tailored for project & service businesses — providing accurate project profitability, multi-currency consolidation, automated workflows, AI-driven forecasting & anomaly detection, global compliance readiness, low-code flexibility, and — with strong integrations — enabling unified financials, resource planning, customer insights, and scalable operations without the complexity or cost of traditional tier-1 ERP systems.
