ControlUp

ControlUp – real-time DEX monitoring, analytics, automation & remediation platform

Overview

ControlUp is a leading **Digital Employee Experience (DEX)** platform that provides real-time visibility, analytics, automated remediation, and proactive optimization across physical endpoints, virtual desktops (VDI), cloud PCs (Windows 365), DaaS environments, and SaaS applications. In March 2026, ControlUp is recognized for its **real-time DEX scoring**, **automated root-cause & remediation**, **employee sentiment & productivity correlation**, **SaaS usage & shadow IT visibility**, **cost optimization** (cloud PC/VDI sizing), **AI-driven insights & recommendations**, and deep support for **Citrix**, **VMware Horizon/Omnissa**, **Azure Virtual Desktop**, **Amazon WorkSpaces**, **Microsoft Intune**, and **Jamf**. It is widely used in VDI/DaaS-heavy organizations and increasingly in hybrid/multi-device enterprises that prioritize end-user experience, ticket deflection, and proactive IT operations.

Key Value: ControlUp delivers real-time, actionable visibility into employee digital experience — enabling proactive remediation, automated issue resolution, reduced support tickets, optimized cloud/VDI costs, improved productivity & sentiment, and — with AI insights — transforming reactive IT support into predictive, user-centric operations.
