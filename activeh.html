ActiveH

ActiveH – cloud-based housing management system for social landlords & asset owners

Overview

ActiveH (ActiveH Web in its current cloud form) is a specialized, integrated housing management platform designed for social landlords, housing associations, local authorities, and asset owners in the regulated housing sector (primarily UK-focused but expanding). In March 2026, ActiveH Web is mature SaaS with strong capabilities in **CRM & tenant management**, **rent & arrears handling**, **asset & repairs management**, **mobile workforce & tasking**, **customer & contractor portals**, **dashboards & analytics**, **compliance automation** (Right to Buy, Universal Credit, equality standards, GDPR), and **sustainability tracking** (energy performance, net zero reporting). It competes with **Northgate Housing**, **Capita ONE Housing**, **Orchard**, **Civica Cx**, and **Nec** in the social housing space but stands out for its **cloud-native evolution**, **automation focus**, **mobile-first approach**, and **tenant experience** emphasis. ActiveH supports end-to-end housing operations from tenancy management to asset lifecycle.

Key Value: ActiveH delivers a modern, cloud-based housing management solution — streamlining rent collection, arrears recovery, repairs & maintenance, tenant communication, asset performance, regulatory compliance, and sustainability reporting — while reducing operational costs, improving tenant satisfaction, enabling mobile working, and providing real-time visibility & dashboards for housing providers and social landlords.
