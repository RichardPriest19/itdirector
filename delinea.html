Delinea

Privileged Access Management – cloud-native identity security and least privilege access

Overview

Delinea is a modern Privileged Access Management (PAM) platform focused on securing identities, credentials, and privileged access across cloud, on-premises, and hybrid environments. In March 2026, Delinea is recognised for its cloud-native architecture, ease of deployment, and strong support for Zero Trust security models. Formed from the merger of Centrify and Thycotic, Delinea provides capabilities including credential vaulting, session monitoring, endpoint privilege management, secrets management, and adaptive access controls. The platform integrates with identity providers, cloud platforms, DevOps pipelines, and security tooling to reduce risk from credential-based attacks and enforce least privilege across human and machine identities.

Key Value: Delinea delivers scalable, cloud-first privileged access management with strong usability and rapid deployment — enabling organisations to enforce least privilege, secure credentials, and protect identities while supporting Zero Trust strategies and modern hybrid environments.
