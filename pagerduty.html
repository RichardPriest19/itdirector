PagerDuty

Incident response and on-call management platform

Overview

PagerDuty is an incident response and on-call management platform helping IT teams and DevOps organizations respond quickly to critical incidents and service outages. PagerDuty automates incident detection, notifies appropriate team members based on schedules and escalation policies, and orchestrates response. In modern DevOps and SRE environments where 24/7 availability is critical, PagerDuty enables teams to respond faster and maintain service reliability. The platform integrates with monitoring tools to automatically trigger incidents when thresholds are exceeded.

Key Value: PagerDuty reduces mean time to response (MTTR) by automating notifications and ensuring the right people are alerted immediately when incidents occur.
