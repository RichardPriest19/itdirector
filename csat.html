CSAT

Customer Satisfaction Score – direct measure of customer happiness after key interactions

Overview

Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT) is a simple, transaction-based metric that asks customers to rate their satisfaction with a specific interaction, product experience, or support ticket (typically on a 1–5 or 1–10 scale). In 2026, CSAT remains a core CX KPI — especially for support, onboarding, and post-interaction touchpoints — but is increasingly complemented by CES (Customer Effort Score) and NPS (Net Promoter Score) for a more complete picture. CSAT is fast to collect, easy to act on, and strongly correlates with churn and renewal behavior in many industries.

Key Value: High and improving CSAT scores predict better retention, higher expansion revenue, lower support costs, and stronger brand advocacy.

CSAT Calculation & Variants

Standard Formula

  • CSAT = (Number of satisfied responses / Total responses) × 100
  • Satisfied = usually 4–5 (on 5-point) or 8–10 (on 10-point)

Common Scales

  • 5-point: Very Dissatisfied → Very Satisfied
  • 10-point: 0–10 numerical scale
  • Emoji-based (for mobile / quick surveys)

Modern CSAT Best Practices (2026)

Collection Strategy

  • Micro-surveys right after key moments (ticket close, onboarding complete)
  • Omnichannel delivery (email, in-app, SMS, WhatsApp)
  • Personalized follow-up for detractors

Action & Closing the Loop

  • Automated alerts for low scores
  • Root-cause analysis via linked comments & ticket data
  • Visible improvement actions shared with customers

Benchmark Targets

  • B2B SaaS: 85–92% (4–5/5 or 8–10/10)
  • Consumer digital: 80–90%
  • High-performing teams target >90% + low detractor %

IT Director Responsibilities

  • Ensure CX platform (Zendesk, Gainsight, Qualtrics, Medallia) is integrated with CRM & support tools
  • Provide real-time CSAT dashboards & drill-down analytics
  • Enable closed-loop feedback automation & alerting
  • Support sentiment analysis & text mining of open comments
  • Monitor CSAT correlation with churn, NRR, expansion
  • Champion voice-of-customer data in product & service roadmap
