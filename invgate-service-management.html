InvGate Service Management

InvGate Service Management – highly configurable ITSM with InvGate AI, asset/CMDB & no-code workflows

Overview

InvGate Service Management is a powerful, cloud-first (with on-premises option) ITSM and enterprise service management platform from InvGate. In March 2026, it stands out for its **deep configurability** (no-code/low-code forms, workflows, portals), **InvGate AI** (virtual agent, ticket summarization, auto-categorization, smart routing), **built-in asset & CMDB**, **project & portfolio management**, **automation engine**, **self-service portal**, **change & release workflows**, **SLA & reporting**, and strong integration with Active Directory, Microsoft 365, Azure AD, Teams, Slack, and third-party tools. It supports ITIL 4 processes and is particularly popular in mid-market, public sector, education, and organizations needing extensive tailoring without premium pricing or vendor lock-in.

Key Value: InvGate Service Management delivers enterprise-grade ITSM with exceptional configurability, robust asset/CMDB, growing InvGate AI intelligence, and excellent value — enabling fast deployment, high customization, strong self-service, and flexible support for IT & non-IT teams at a competitive price point.
