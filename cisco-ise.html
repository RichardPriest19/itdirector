Cisco ISE

Cisco Identity Services Engine – leading NAC, zero-trust access & policy platform

Overview

Cisco Identity Services Engine (ISE) is the market-leading Network Access Control (NAC) and policy platform that enforces identity-based access, device profiling, posture assessment, guest/BYOD onboarding, segmentation, and zero-trust security across wired, wireless, and VPN networks. In March 2026, **ISE 3.4 / 3.5** is the current production standard (with 3.6 in preview), deeply integrated into the **Cisco Security Cloud** ecosystem — powering **Cisco Secure Access**, **SD-Access**, **TrustSec macro/micro-segmentation**, **pxGrid 2.0** for ecosystem sharing, **AI-driven profiling & risk scoring**, **posture remediation**, **IoT/OT visibility**, **zero-trust segmentation**, **regulatory alignment** (DORA, NIS2, PCI-DSS, HIPAA), and tight synergy with **Duo** (MFA), **Umbrella** (DNS security), **SecureX** (orchestration), **Catalyst** switches, **Meraki**, and **ThousandEyes**. ISE is deployed in the vast majority of large enterprises and critical infrastructure organizations as the policy decision point for network access and segmentation.

Key Value: Cisco ISE delivers identity-centric, context-aware network access control — enabling zero-trust enforcement, dynamic segmentation, device profiling & posture checks, secure guest/BYOD onboarding, automated compliance, AI-enhanced risk decisions, and — when integrated with Cisco Secure Access & SD-Access — providing end-to-end zero-trust, regulatory alignment, reduced attack surface, and operational efficiency across wired, wireless, VPN, and IoT/OT environments.
