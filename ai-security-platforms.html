AI Security Platforms

Dedicated platforms to secure AI models, pipelines, and applications against prompt injection, model poisoning, data leakage, and supply-chain risks

Overview

AI Security Platforms provide centralized governance, runtime protection, vulnerability scanning for models, and guardrails for generative and agentic AI systems. They address the unique attack surface created by large language models and AI-native applications.

Key Features

  • Prompt injection & jailbreak prevention
  • Model poisoning and adversarial attack detection
  • Data leakage & PII protection in inference
  • Supply-chain security for training data and models
  • Policy enforcement, auditing, and compliance reporting

Leading Approaches (2026)

Platforms increasingly integrate with MLOps pipelines and offer agentic security layers. Look for solutions that combine runtime shielding with governance dashboards and support for both on-premises and cloud AI workloads.
