Freshdesk

Freshdesk – cloud-based helpdesk & customer support platform with Freddy AI & omnichannel capabilities

Overview

Freshdesk is a modern, cloud-native customer support and helpdesk platform (part of Freshworks Customer Service Suite) designed for fast-growing teams and enterprises. In March 2026, it stands out for **Freddy AI** — a generative AI engine that powers ticket summarization, smart replies, intent classification, auto-triage, knowledge base suggestions, and CSAT prediction. Freshdesk offers omnichannel support (email, chat, WhatsApp, voice, social, self-service portal), **Freshdesk Omni** intelligent routing, advanced automation (Freddy Workflow), robust analytics, SLAs, team collaboration, and seamless integration with Freshsales CRM, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Slack, WhatsApp Business, and telephony providers. It balances ease of use for agents with enterprise-grade security, compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2), and scalability.

Key Value: Freshdesk delivers fast, AI-powered support that reduces resolution time, improves CSAT & agent productivity, scales omnichannel experiences, and provides actionable insights — making it ideal for IT service desks, customer support teams, and businesses prioritizing customer experience in a digital-first world.
