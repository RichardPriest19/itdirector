Gong

Gong – AI-powered revenue intelligence & conversation analytics platform

Overview

Gong is the leading revenue intelligence platform that captures, transcribes, analyzes, and activates customer conversations across calls (Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, Webex, dialers), emails, chat (Slack), and CRM notes. In March 2026, Gong leverages **generative AI** (Gong Copilot, Gong Engage, Gong Insights) for **conversation summaries**, **deal intelligence**, **forecast accuracy**, **coaching & playbook automation**, **sentiment & behavior analysis**, **deal risk scoring**, **win/loss intelligence**, **CRM enrichment** (Salesforce, HubSpot, Dynamics 365), and **revenue orchestration**. It offers **enterprise-grade governance** (SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, HIPAA, data residency, role-based access), and is widely adopted in B2B sales, customer success, marketing, and RevOps teams to improve win rates, shorten sales cycles, increase forecast accuracy, and scale coaching.

Key Value: Gong turns every customer conversation into actionable revenue intelligence — using AI to uncover deal risks, coach reps, improve forecasting, automate CRM updates, and accelerate revenue growth while maintaining enterprise compliance and data security.
