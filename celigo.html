Celigo

Integration Platform as a Service (iPaaS) for connecting NetSuite and cloud applications

Overview

Celigo is a leading Integration Platform as a Service (iPaaS) designed to connect NetSuite with a wide range of cloud and on-premises applications. In March 2026, Celigo is widely used for pre-built and custom integrations across ERP, CRM, eCommerce, finance, and operational systems. Its Integration Apps (prebuilt connectors) and integrator.io platform enable rapid deployment, low-code development, and scalable integration management. Celigo supports real-time and batch data synchronisation, API orchestration, error handling, and monitoring — making it a key component of modern API-led and event-driven architectures.

Key Value: Celigo accelerates integration delivery through prebuilt connectors and low-code tooling — enabling organisations to connect systems quickly, reduce development effort, and maintain scalable, governed integrations across their digital ecosystem.
