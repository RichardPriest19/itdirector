Looker

Looker (Google Cloud) – semantic modeling, governed BI, embedded analytics & AI-powered insights

Overview

Looker (Google Cloud) is Google’s enterprise business intelligence and data platform, centered on **LookML** — a semantic modeling language that creates a single source of truth for business metrics and definitions. In March 2026 (Looker 24.x / 25.x release cycle), it features **Gemini in Looker** for natural language querying, AI-assisted modeling, automated insights & explanations, **Looker Studio** (free visualization layer), deep BigQuery integration, embedded analytics, and governed self-service at scale. Looker excels in delivering consistent, trusted metrics across the organization while supporting real-time data exploration, custom applications, and integration with Google Cloud ecosystem (BigQuery, Dataform, Vertex AI, AlloyDB).

Key Value: Looker provides governed, reusable metrics via LookML, AI-powered exploration (Gemini), fast performance on BigQuery, and flexible embedding — enabling consistent analytics, reduced shadow BI, and high trust across business and technical users.
