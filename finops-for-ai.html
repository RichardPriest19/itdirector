FinOps for AI

Cloud financial operations tailored to the unique economics of AI workloads – GPU utilization, inference optimization, and sustainable scaling

Overview

Traditional FinOps focuses on IaaS/PaaS cost visibility. FinOps for AI extends this to track and optimize expensive GPU/TPU usage, model training vs. inference costs, spot/preemptible instances, and energy consumption in AI pipelines.

Key Metrics to Track

  • GPU utilization and idle time
  • Cost per inference / cost per token
  • Training run efficiency and checkpointing savings
  • Carbon-aware scheduling and sustainable AI practices
  • Model right-sizing and quantization benefits

Best Practices 2026

  • Tag AI resources by model, team, and use case
  • Implement automated shutdown for idle training jobs
  • Use reserved capacity or savings plans for predictable inference
  • Integrate with existing FinOps tools and showback/chargeback models
