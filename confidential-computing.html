Confidential Computing

Hardware-based Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs) that protect data in use – critical for secure AI inference and regulated workloads

Overview

Confidential Computing uses CPU features (Intel TDX, AMD SEV, ARM CCA, etc.) and confidential VMs/containers to keep data encrypted even while being processed. It is rapidly becoming a foundational technology for enterprise AI adoption in 2026.

Use Cases

  • Secure multi-party AI training and inference
  • Protecting sensitive data in cloud/hybrid environments
  • Regulated industries (finance, healthcare, government)
  • Confidential AI model serving without exposing weights or prompts

2026 Status

75%+ of organizations are adopting or piloting confidential computing. Gartner ranks it among the top technologies to watch. Major cloud providers offer confidential VM and container options with attestation capabilities.
