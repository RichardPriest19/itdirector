Digital Provenance

Verifiable tracking of content origin, edits, and AI generation using C2PA and related standards – essential for trust in the age of generative AI

Overview

Digital Provenance provides cryptographically signed records (Content Credentials) that document who created content, what tools were used, and whether AI was involved. The C2PA standard is the leading open specification.

Why It Matters in 2026

With generative AI producing vast amounts of content, provenance combats deepfakes, supports regulatory compliance (including EU AI Act transparency obligations), and restores trust in digital media.

Key Standard: C2PA

Content Credentials capture creation, editing history, and AI usage. Supported by Adobe, Microsoft, Google, OpenAI, and thousands of organizations. Implementations now appear in cameras, creative tools, and publishing platforms.
