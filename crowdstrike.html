CrowdStrike

CrowdStrike Falcon – cloud-native endpoint protection, EDR/XDR & threat hunting platform

Overview

CrowdStrike is a leading cloud-native cybersecurity company best known for its **Falcon platform** — a unified, AI-powered solution delivering next-generation antivirus (NGAV), endpoint detection & response (EDR), extended detection & response (XDR), managed threat hunting, identity protection, cloud security, exposure management, and SOAR capabilities. In March 2026, Falcon is widely regarded as the gold standard for endpoint security and modern SOC operations — with **Falcon Prevent** (NGAV), **Falcon Insight XDR** (EDR/XDR), **Falcon OverWatch** (24/7 managed hunting), **Falcon Complete** (MDR), **Charlotte AI** (generative AI assistant for analysts), **Falcon Fusion** (SOAR), **Falcon Identity Protection**, **Falcon Cloud Security**, **Falcon Exposure Management** (attack surface & cyber asset management), **Falcon LogScale** (next-gen SIEM), and **Falcon Adversary OverWatch** (nation-state & ransomware intelligence). It is heavily used for **threat hunting**, **incident response**, **zero-trust endpoint posture**, **AI-powered behavioral detection**, **ransomware prevention**, **supply-chain risk visibility**, and **regulatory compliance** (DORA, NIS2, SEC 4-hour reporting, CMMC 2.0). CrowdStrike is deployed in the vast majority of Fortune 500 companies and integrated with major SIEMs, SOARs, and identity platforms.

Key Value: CrowdStrike Falcon delivers cloud-native, AI-driven endpoint & cloud protection with exceptional detection efficacy, rapid response, managed hunting, identity & exposure visibility, and — through Charlotte AI & Falcon Fusion — dramatically reduces analyst workload, MTTD/MTTR, alert fatigue, and ransomware impact while providing defensible, auditable security posture for compliance & insurance requirements.
