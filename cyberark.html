CyberArk

Privileged Access Management – securing identities, credentials, and critical systems

Overview

CyberArk is a leading Privileged Access Management (PAM) platform designed to secure, manage, and monitor privileged accounts across enterprise IT environments. In March 2026, CyberArk is a core component of Zero Trust security strategies, protecting critical systems, cloud environments, DevOps pipelines, and identities from credential theft and misuse. The platform provides capabilities including secure credential vaulting, session monitoring, least privilege enforcement, secrets management, and identity security across hybrid and multi-cloud environments. CyberArk integrates with identity providers, SIEM platforms, endpoint security tools, and DevOps workflows to deliver comprehensive protection against insider threats and external attacks.

Key Value: CyberArk reduces the risk of credential-based attacks by securing privileged identities and enforcing least privilege access — enabling organisations to protect critical assets, achieve compliance, and support Zero Trust architectures while maintaining operational efficiency and visibility.
